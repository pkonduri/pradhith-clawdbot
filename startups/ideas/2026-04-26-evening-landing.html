How It Works

From field to exchange in four steps.

1

Deploy Sensors

Solar-powered SoilPods are installed across the farm. No wiring, no drilling — just push in and go.

2

Monitor Continuously

Sensors measure carbon levels, moisture, nutrients, and microbial activity 24/7. Satellites verify from above.

3

Verify & Grade

Our AI engine calculates actual carbon sequestration and assigns a credit grade (AAA to C) based on permanence.

Credits automatically list on the exchange. Farmers get paid instantly when buyers purchase.
