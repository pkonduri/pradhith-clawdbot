# DealRoom AI — Autonomous M&A Due Diligence Intelligence

**One-liner:** The AI operating system that transforms weeks of M&A due diligence into hours, catching what humans miss.

**Category:** Enterprise SaaS / FinTech / LegalTech  
**Stage:** Pre-seed / Seed ready  
**Date:** March 9, 2026

---

## The Problem

M&A due diligence is broken:

- **Time:** Average deal requires 4-8 weeks of due diligence, reviewing 10,000+ documents
- **Cost:** Large deals spend $2-5M on DD alone (legal, financial, operational reviews)
- **Risk:** Human reviewers miss critical issues — 40% of deals fail to deliver expected value
- **Bottleneck:** Limited expert availability delays deals, costing millions in opportunity cost
- **Fragmentation:** Legal, financial, HR, IP, and operational DD happen in silos

The M&A market is worth **$5+ trillion annually**, with due diligence representing a **$50B+ market**. Yet the process hasn't fundamentally changed in decades.

### Why Now?

1. **AI Maturity:** LLMs can finally understand complex legal and financial documents
2. **Deal Volume Surge:** Private equity dry powder at all-time highs ($2.5T+)
3. **Speed Pressure:** Competitive processes demand faster DD without sacrificing quality
4. **Regulatory Complexity:** Cross-border deals face exponentially more compliance requirements
5. **Risk Awareness:** Post-pandemic, buyers demand deeper operational DD

### Key Features

**1. Universal Document Intelligence**
- Ingest any format: PDFs, contracts, financials, emails, data rooms
- Multi-language support for cross-border deals
- OCR for scanned documents, handwritten notes
- Automatic document classification and organization

**2. Deep Contract Analytics**
- Extract every obligation, right, and restriction
- Identify change of control provisions
- Map MAC clauses and carve-outs
- Analyze indemnification structures
- Flag unusual or non-standard terms

**3. Financial Forensics**
- Quality of earnings analysis
- Working capital normalization
- Revenue recognition pattern detection
- Related party transaction identification
- EBITDA adjustment validation

**4. Risk Scoring Engine**
- Proprietary deal risk score (0-100)
- Category-specific risk breakdowns
- Benchmark against comparable transactions
- Predictive integration risk assessment

**5. Knowledge Graph**
- Map all entities, relationships, and obligations
- Visualize corporate structure complexity
- Identify hidden liabilities and contingencies
- Track beneficial ownership chains

**6. AI Deal Negotiator**
- Generate negotiation points from findings
- Draft rep & warranty language
- Suggest purchase price adjustments
- Create closing condition checklists
