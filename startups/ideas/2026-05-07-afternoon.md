## How It Works

### Core Platform Components

#### 1. SpatialStudio — The Creation Engine

The AI-native creation environment where spatial experiences come to life.

**Text-to-Space Generation:**
```
User: "Create an immersive product demo for our new electric vehicle. 
       Show the exterior from all angles, let users open doors and sit 
       inside, and provide interactive specs."

SpatialForge: Generates complete spatial experience with:
- Photorealistic 3D model of vehicle
- Animated doors, trunk, hood
- Interactive hotspots with specifications
- Ambient environment with reflections
- Spatial audio (engine sound, door sounds)
- Optimized for Vision Pro and Quest 3
```

**Visual Editing Canvas:**
- Drag-and-drop spatial elements
- Real-time 3D preview with VR mode
- Component library of pre-built interactions
- AI assistant for continuous refinement

**Asset Generation Pipeline:**
- Text-to-3D model generation
- Image-to-3D reconstruction
- Style transfer and material application
- Automatic LOD generation for performance

No-code interaction design that makes spatial experiences truly interactive.

- AI-suggested interaction patterns

**Physics & Simulation:**
- Realistic object physics
- Spatial audio integration
- Haptic feedback mapping
- Environmental effects

**Multi-User Capabilities:**
- Built-in multiplayer support
- Avatar systems
- Spatial voice chat
- Collaborative annotations

#### 3. SpatialDeploy — Universal Delivery

One-click deployment to every platform.

**Platform Targets:**

- Mobile AR (iOS ARKit, Android ARCore)

**Optimization Engine:**

**App Store Pathways:**

#### 4. SpatialAnalytics — Engagement Intelligence

Understand how users interact with spatial content.

**Spatial Heatmaps:**

- Time spent in areas

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Session duration
- Interaction depth

**A/B Testing:**
- Test spatial layouts
