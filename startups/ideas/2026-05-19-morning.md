# Provenance.ai вҖ” The AI Trust Layer for High-Value Asset Authentication & Intelligence

**The Problem That's About to Explode**

By 2026, AI-generated forgeries have become indistinguishable from authentic works. A single Midjourney-generated "Basquiat" sold for $2.1M before detection. The FBI reports a 340% increase in luxury goods counterfeiting using generative AI. The $68B art market, $350B luxury goods market, and $80B collectibles market face an existential trust crisis.

Traditional authentication relies on:
- Human experts (scarce, expensive, inconsistent)
- Paper certificates (easily forged)
- Siloed databases (fragmented, incomplete)
- Reactive detection (too late)

**The Provenance.ai Solution**

An end-to-end AI-powered trust infrastructure that:
1. **Authenticates** assets using multimodal AI analysis
2. **Tracks** provenance on immutable blockchain ledger
3. **Predicts** value with market intelligence
4. **Enables** secure transactions and fractional ownership
