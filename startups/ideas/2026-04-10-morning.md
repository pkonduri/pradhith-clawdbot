# AirSentry — The AI Operating System for Indoor Environment Intelligence

## Executive Summary

AirSentry is the autonomous intelligence layer for indoor air quality, transforming buildings from passive structures into health-optimizing environments. Using distributed AI-powered sensors and predictive analytics, AirSentry detects invisible threats — pathogens, allergens, toxins, and air quality degradations — before they impact human health and productivity.

**The Big Bet:** Indoor air is the next frontier of preventive healthcare. AirSentry doesn't just monitor air — it actively protects occupants, optimizes environments for human performance, and creates the "immune system" for buildings.

---

## The Problem

### The Invisible Health Crisis

Humans spend **90% of their lives indoors**, yet indoor environments remain largely unmonitored and unoptimized:

- **Indoor air is 2-5x more polluted** than outdoor air (EPA)
- **$150B+** annual healthcare costs from indoor air pollution in the US alone
- **4.3M deaths globally** from indoor air pollution annually (WHO)
- **$38B** lost annually in US productivity due to poor indoor air quality
- **50% of commercial buildings** have IAQ problems (NIOSH)
- **67%** of building occupants report "sick building syndrome" symptoms

### The Post-Pandemic Reality

COVID-19 permanently changed how we think about indoor air:

- **Aerosol transmission** is now widely understood
- **HVAC systems** are recognized as infection vectors
- **Building liability** for air quality has skyrocketed
- **$25B+ HVAC retrofit market** emerged
- **Workers demand** healthy buildings before returning to offices
- **"Air quality" searches** up 340% since 2020

### The Wildfire Multiplier

Climate change is making indoor air protection critical:

- **100M Americans** affected by wildfire smoke annually (growing 8% YoY)
- **Smoke events** lasting 2-4x longer than a decade ago
- **Indoor PM2.5 levels** can spike 10x during smoke events
- **Healthcare costs** from wildfire smoke: $16B annually
- **Schools close** during smoke events — billions in economic impact

### Why Current Solutions Fail

| Current Approach | Reality |
|-----------------|---------|
| Single-room purifiers | Isolated, no intelligence, no health correlation |
| Basic HVAC filters | Static, changed reactively, no monitoring |
| CO₂ monitors | One dimension of a multi-factor problem |
| Building management systems | Focus on energy, not health |
| Industrial IAQ sensors | Expensive ($10K+), complex, no actionable insights |
| Consumer air quality apps | Data without action, outdoor-focused |

### The Business Pain Points

**For Commercial Real Estate:**
- Class A office vacancy at 18%+ — IAQ is a differentiation opportunity
- Tenant lawsuits for health issues increasing 300%
- ESG investors demanding health metrics
- Insurance premiums rising for poor IAQ

**For Enterprises:**
- Average employee loses 4 productive hours/week to poor air quality
- Sick leave costs $225B annually — 30% linked to indoor environment
- Talent expects healthy workplaces post-pandemic
- Presenteeism (working while unwell) costs 10x absenteeism

**For Schools & Healthcare:**
- 50% of US schools have poor IAQ

- Regulatory pressure intensifying
