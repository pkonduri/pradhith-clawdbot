### Product Suite

#### 1. DesignAI — Generative Transformer Engineering
**The Problem:** Every transformer is custom-engineered from scratch. A single design takes 4-8 weeks of specialized engineering time.

**The Solution:**
- **Foundation model trained on 100,000+ transformer designs** spanning voltage classes, applications, and regulatory requirements
- **Instant topology optimization:** Input specs → optimized design in hours, not weeks
- **Automated compliance validation:** IEEE C57, IEC 60076, UL standards pre-validated
- **Material substitution intelligence:** When copper is scarce, automatically redesign for aluminum alternatives
- **Manufacturing-aware design:** Designs optimized for our modular production system

**Impact:** 90% reduction in engineering time. Weeks → hours.

#### 2. ModularCore — Prefabricated Manufacturing System
**The Problem:** Traditional transformers are built as monolithic units. Each one is a custom project.

**The Solution:**
- **Standardized core modules:** 80% of transformer volume covered by 12 base configurations
- **Snap-together assembly:** Modular windings, cores, and enclosures
- **Regional microfactories:** 50,000 sq ft facilities vs. 500,000 sq ft legacy plants
- **Automated winding systems:** AI-controlled robotic winding reduces labor 75%
- **Parallel production:** Build modules simultaneously, assemble on-site

**Impact:** 
- Lead time: 3 years → 6 months (distribution), 4 years → 12 months (power)
- Cost: 30% reduction through standardization
- Capacity: 10x throughput per factory square foot

#### 3. GridMind — Predictive Maintenance & Fleet Intelligence
**The Problem:** 70% of grid transformers are over 25 years old. Utilities don't know which will fail next.

**The Solution:**
- **IoT sensor retrofit kits** for existing transformer fleets
- **AI failure prediction:** 12-month advance warning on critical failures
- **Optimized replacement scheduling:** Batch orders, prioritize high-risk assets
- **Digital twin tracking:** Every transformer we sell has a lifetime digital model
- **Performance optimization:** Real-time load balancing recommendations

**Impact:** 
- Prevent catastrophic failures (single large transformer failure = $10M+ damage + outages)
- Enable proactive replacement vs. emergency scrambles
- Extend transformer life 20-30% through optimized operation
