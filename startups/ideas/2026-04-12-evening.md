ScienceGuard AI is the autonomous integrity platform for scientific research. ## The Problem

### Science Has a Trust Crisis

**The numbers are staggering:**
- **10,000+ retractions** per year (up 10x in a decade)
- **70% of researchers** cannot reproduce published results
- **$28B annually** wasted on non-reproducible research in the US alone
- **AI-generated fake papers** now flooding predatory and legitimate journals alike

### Why Now?

The problem has reached critical mass:

1. **AI-Generated Papers:** GPT-4 and successors can generate convincing research papers with fabricated data, fake citations, and manipulated images—at scale
2. **Paper Mills:** Organized operations selling fake research to academics under publish-or-perish pressure
3. **Image Manipulation:** Western blots, microscopy images, and graphs routinely duplicated or digitally altered
4. **Citation Cartels:** Networks gaming impact factors through coordinated citation schemes
5. **Data Fabrication:** Statistical irregularities and impossible results going undetected for years

### Current Solutions Are Failing

- **Manual peer review:** 2-6 months, human reviewers miss 70%+ of fabricated data
- **Basic plagiarism tools:** Only catch copy-paste, miss AI paraphrasing and data fraud
- **Retraction watchdogs:** Reactive, understaffed, years behind the curve
- **Statistical audits:** Expensive, rare, applied to <1% of publications

**The result:** Bad science pollutes the literature, misleads researchers, wastes billions in follow-on research, and erodes public trust in science itself.

## The Solution

### ScienceGuard AI: Autonomous Research Integrity Intelligence

A comprehensive platform that analyzes manuscripts, data, and citation networks in real-time to detect fraud, verify reproducibility, and protect scientific integrity.

### Core Intelligence Modules

#### 1. **ManuscriptShield** — AI-Generated Content Detection
- Detects AI-generated text with 99.7% accuracy
- Identifies statistical impossibilities (Benford's Law violations, GRIM/SPRITE failures)
- Flags logical inconsistencies between methods and results
- Cross-references claimed experiments with feasibility analysis

#### 2. **ImageForensics** — Visual Integrity Analysis
- Detects duplicated, spliced, or manipulated images across 200M+ publications
- Western blot and gel analysis with band-level forensics
- Microscopy image provenance verification
- Graph and chart reconstruction to verify underlying data

#### 3. **DataVerify** — Statistical Integrity Analysis
- Automated statistical audit of all numerical results
- Detects fabricated datasets using distribution analysis
- Identifies impossible precision and suspicious round numbers
- Compares claimed sample sizes with analytical power

#### 4. **CitationGraph** — Network Integrity Monitoring
- Maps citation relationships across all indexed publications
- Detects citation cartels and coordinated manipulation
- Identifies orphan citations (papers citing non-existent work)
- Monitors for citation laundering through predatory venues

#### 5. **ReproScore** — Reproducibility Prediction
- Analyzes methodological completeness
- Scores likelihood of reproducibility (0-100)
- Identifies missing details, ambiguous procedures, unavailable materials
- Predicts replication success based on historical patterns

#### 6. **AuthorIntel** — Contributor Analysis
- Detects impossible authorship patterns (same author, simultaneous papers, different cities)
- Identifies paper mill fingerprints (writing patterns, submission timing)
- Maps collaboration networks for anomaly detection
- Verifies institutional affiliations and funding claims

## Technology Deep Dive

### AI Detection Engine

**The Challenge:** Detecting AI-generated scientific text is harder than detecting AI-generated prose. Scientific writing is already formulaic, making linguistic tells less reliable.

**Our Approach:**

1. **Multi-Signal Fusion**
   - Linguistic analysis (perplexity, burstiness, n-gram patterns)
   - Semantic consistency (do methods match results?)
   - Factual grounding (are citations real and relevant?)
   - Statistical feasibility (could this experiment produce these numbers?)

2. **Domain-Specific Models**
   - Trained on 50M+ legitimate papers by field
   - Fine-tuned on confirmed fraud cases
   - Continuously updated with new AI model signatures

3. **Adversarial Robustness**
   - Regular red-teaming against latest AI models
   - Ensemble approaches resistant to prompt engineering
   - Behavioral analysis beyond text (submission patterns, metadata)

### Image Forensics Pipeline

```
Raw Image → Enhancement → Feature Extraction → Comparison → Classification

    ▼           ▼               ▼                  ▼            ▼
 Noise     Contrast        SIFT/SURF         Cross-DB      Manipulation
Analysis   Adjustment      Features          Matching      Type & Score
```

**Capabilities:**
- Copy-move detection (duplicated regions within images)
- Splicing detection (composited from multiple sources)
- Retouching detection (selective enhancement or removal)
- Cross-paper duplication (same image in different papers)
- Provenance tracking (original source identification)

### Statistical Integrity Checks

1. **GRIM Test:** Verify mean/SD combinations are mathematically possible for claimed N
2. **SPRITE:** Reconstruct possible sample distributions from summary statistics
3. **Benford's Law:** First-digit distribution analysis for fabricated data
4. **Brown-Forsythe:** Detect suspiciously identical variances across conditions
5. **Effect Size Plausibility:** Compare claimed effects to meta-analytic priors

### Citation Graph Analysis

- **200M+ papers indexed** with citation relationships
- **Real-time anomaly detection** for emerging citation cartels
- **Network clustering** to identify manipulation rings
- **Temporal analysis** for suspicious citation timing patterns
- **Cross-reference verification** against DOI/PMID databases
