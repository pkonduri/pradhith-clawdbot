Static Protocol Design

Protocols written once, never adapted. Eligibility criteria exclude 80% of patients unnecessarily. No optimization as data emerges mid-trial.

40% avoidable protocol failures

Weeks of detection delay

Six AI Engines. One Platform.

TrialOS brings autonomous intelligence to every stage of clinical research.

🎯

PatientMatch Engine

Federated analysis across EHR systems, claims data, and registries. Dynamic eligibility optimization. Predictive enrollment forecasting.

95% enrollment targets achieved
🧠

ProtocolMind Engine

AI protocol generation with scientific justification. Bayesian adaptive design. Real-time dose optimization and endpoint evolution.

40% fewer trial failures

SafetyNet Engine
