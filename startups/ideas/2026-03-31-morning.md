## The Problem

### The AI Agent Explosion

By 2026, enterprises are projected to deploy 10-100x more AI agents than human employees. But while companies have sophisticated HR systems for human workers, they have **nothing** for AI workers:

- **Discovery Chaos:** 500+ agent vendors, no standardized way to evaluate capabilities
- **Procurement Hell:** Each agent has different pricing, terms, API requirements
- **No Onboarding:** Integrating agents into workflows requires custom engineering every time
- **Zero Performance Management:** No unified dashboards, no KPIs, no accountability
- **Cost Explosion:** Shadow AI spending is uncontrolled, with massive redundancy
- **Compliance Risk:** Which agents have access to what data? Who approved them?
