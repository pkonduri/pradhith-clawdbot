The Problem

The Diagnostic Odyssey is Healthcare's Cruelest Journey

For 400 million people with rare diseases, getting a diagnosis is a nightmare of misdiagnoses, endless specialist visits, and years of uncertainty.

⏳

Years of Waiting

Patients see an average of 8+ specialists over 5-7 years before receiving a correct diagnosis.

5-7 year average
❌

Misdiagnoses

The average rare disease patient receives 2-3 incorrect diagnoses before finding the truth.

2-3 wrong diagnoses
💸

Massive Costs

Families spend tens of thousands on unnecessary tests and treatments while searching for answers.

$1T annual US cost
🧩

Knowledge Fragmentation

No single doctor can know 7,000+ rare diseases. Critical knowledge is scattered across specialists worldwide.

7,000+ diseases
🚫

No Treatments

95% of rare diseases have no FDA-approved treatment. Patients are often left without options.

95% untreatable
👶

Children Suffer Most

Half of rare disease patients are children. 30% won't live to see their 5th birthday.

50% are children
