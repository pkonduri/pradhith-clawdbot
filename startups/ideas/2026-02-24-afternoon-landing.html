The Problem

Edge AI is Broken

Billions of devices need to run AI locally. But the infrastructure to make this happen simply doesn't exist.

🧩

Fragmented Hardware

Dozens of chip architectures with no unified runtime. Qualcomm, Apple, NVIDIA, Intel, custom ASICs—each needs different code.

🔧

Deployment Hell

Teams spend months manually optimizing models for each device. Quantization, pruning, format conversion—all done by hand.

👁️

Zero Fleet Visibility

No way to monitor AI performance across millions of devices. No A/B testing. No rollback. Flying blind.

⚡

Latency vs. Quality

No intelligent routing between edge and cloud. Static decisions that don't adapt to context, battery, or network.

🔒

Compliance Blind Spots

Edge processing lacks audit trails. GDPR, HIPAA, EU AI Act compliance is an afterthought.

💸

Wasted Engineering

Every company rebuilds the same edge infrastructure from scratch. Millions in duplicated effort.
