## The $200 Billion Opportunity

Every year, humanity generates **62 million tons of e-waste** — more than the weight of the Great Wall of China. Only 22% gets recycled. The rest? Landfills, incinerators, or shipped to developing nations.

Meanwhile:
- **$200B worth of raw materials** discarded annually in e-waste
- **78% of consumers** want to repair rather than replace
- **Right-to-repair laws** passed in 30+ states, EU mandates effective 2025
- **Average device lifespan** dropped 40% in the last decade
- **Repair industry** fragmented, underskilled, and analog

The right-to-repair movement won the legislative battle. Now we need the infrastructure.

**RepairOS is the AI-native operating system that makes device repair accessible to everyone — from consumers fixing their own phones to certified technicians running repair businesses.**

## The Problem: Repair Is Broken

### For Consumers
- **No diagnosis** — is it the battery, screen, motherboard? Who knows
- **No parts access** — OEM locks, proprietary screws, glued components
- **No guidance** — YouTube videos are hit-or-miss, often outdated
- **No trust** — local repair shops are a gamble
- **No economics** — repair often costs more than replacement

### For Repair Technicians
- **Manual diagnosis** — hours of troubleshooting per device
- **Parts sourcing nightmare** — thousands of SKUs, quality varies wildly
- **No documentation** — schematics are hoarded by OEMs
- **Skills gap** — new devices launch faster than training
- **Zero scale** — every repair is a one-off

### For Manufacturers
- **Right-to-repair compliance** — legal mandates without infrastructure
- **Sustainability reporting** — ESG pressure, no tools to track
- **Extended producer responsibility** — take-back requirements growing
- **Brand reputation** — "planned obsolescence" backlash hurting sales

### Why Now?
1. **Regulatory tsunami** — EU Ecodesign, US right-to-repair laws forcing OEM compliance
2. **AI capability** — vision models can now diagnose hardware issues from photos/videos
3. **Sustainability deadlines** — 2030 commitments demand circular solutions
4. **Consumer shift** — Gen Z values repair, resale, sustainability over new
5. **Parts ecosystem** — aftermarket parts quality dramatically improved

## Core Platform

### 1. DiagnoseAI — Instant Device Diagnosis

**Visual AI Diagnosis:**
- Point your phone camera at any device
- AI identifies make, model, year automatically
- Analyzes symptoms from video (flickering screens, boot loops, physical damage)
- Returns ranked probable causes with confidence scores

**Conversational Diagnosis:**
- "My laptop won't turn on, the charging light blinks twice"
- AI asks clarifying questions like a master technician
- Cross-references millions of repair cases
- Outputs diagnosis with 94% accuracy

**Sound-Based Analysis:**
- Upload audio of clicking hard drives, buzzing power supplies
- AI identifies failure modes from acoustic signatures
- Detects problems before they become catastrophic

**Sensor Integration:**
- Connects to device diagnostics (battery health, storage SMART data)
- Pulls error codes from system logs
- Correlates hardware and software issues

### 2. RepairGuide AI — Interactive Repair Instructions

**Dynamic Step-by-Step:**
- AI-generated repair guides for 50,000+ device models
- Adapts in real-time based on your progress
- Warns before common mistakes
- Adjusts difficulty for skill level

**AR Overlay Mode:**
- Point phone at device, see AR overlay of next step
- Highlights exact screws, connectors, components
- Shows safe pry points for adhesive removal
- Real-time hazard warnings (battery puncture zones)

**Video Companion:**
- AI watches your repair via camera
- Corrects mistakes before they happen
- "Stop — that's the display connector, not the battery"
- Celebrates successful steps (dopamine hits matter)

**Tool & Part Identification:**
- Scans your toolkit, recommends what you need
- Shows exact bit types, sizes, quantities
- Links to purchase for missing tools

### 3. PartSource AI — Universal Parts Marketplace

**Intelligent Parts Matching:**
- Input device model, get all compatible parts instantly
- Cross-references OEM, aftermarket, refurbished options
- Quality scores based on supplier history
- Price comparison across 200+ vendors

**Quality Assurance:**
- AI verifies seller authenticity
- Analyzes part photos for defects
- Predicts failure rates by vendor/batch
- Money-back guarantee on AI-verified parts

**Harvest Network:**
- Connects repair shops with e-waste sources
- AI identifies salvageable parts from broken devices
- Creates liquid market for harvested components
- Reduces virgin material demand 40%

**Inventory Intelligence:**
- For repair businesses: predicts parts demand
- Auto-reorders based on repair trends
- Seasonal adjustments (phone drops spike in summer)
- Dead stock alerts and liquidation recommendations

### 4. TechSkill AI — Repair Workforce Training

**Adaptive Learning:**
- Personalized curriculum based on skill gaps
- Simulated repairs in VR/AR before touching real devices
- Certification paths (basic → advanced → master)
- Specialization tracks (mobile, laptop, appliance, EV)

**On-the-Job Coaching:**
- AI mentor watches live repairs
- Suggests techniques in real-time
- Captures best practices from top technicians
- Creates training content automatically

**Certification & Credentialing:**
- Industry-recognized certifications
- Verified repair history as portable credential
- Technician ratings and specializations
- Background checks and insurance verification

### 5. BusinessOS — Repair Shop Operations

**End-to-End Shop Management:**
- POS integrated with diagnosis and parts
- Customer device intake with AI triage
- Automated quotes based on AI diagnosis
- Repair tracking and customer communication

**Pricing Intelligence:**
- Dynamic pricing based on part costs, labor, market rates
- Profit margin optimization
- Competitive analysis by geography
- Warranty cost predictions

**Quality Control:**
- Post-repair AI inspection
- Catches common oversights (missing screws, loose connectors)
- Customer satisfaction prediction
- Warranty claim reduction
