### Product Pillars

#### 1. PatientMatch Engine — AI-Powered Recruitment

**Problem:** Finding eligible, willing, accessible patients is the #1 trial killer.

**Solution:**
- **Federated Health Graph**: Privacy-preserving analysis across EHR systems, claims data, genomic databases, and patient registries
- **Dynamic Eligibility Optimization**: AI identifies overly restrictive criteria that exclude patients without scientific reason
- **Predictive Enrollment**: Forecasts enrollment velocity by site, geography, and patient segment
- **Patient Engagement AI**: Personalized outreach, consent explanation, and retention nudges
- **Decentralized Trial Support**: Matches patients to remote monitoring when site visits aren't necessary

**Impact:** 95% enrollment target achievement. 60% faster recruitment. 40% cost reduction.

#### 2. ProtocolMind Engine — Adaptive Trial Design

**Problem:** Static protocols can't adapt to emerging data.

**Solution:**
- **AI Protocol Generation**: Given therapeutic target and patient population, generates optimal protocol with scientific justification
- **Bayesian Adaptive Design**: Real-time dose optimization, arm selection, and sample size re-estimation
- **Endpoint Evolution**: Suggests protocol amendments when primary endpoints show insufficient signal
- **Cross-Trial Learning**: Synthesizes insights from similar historical trials to predict failure modes
- **Regulatory Pre-Check**: Validates protocol against FDA/EMA requirements before submission

**Impact:** 40% reduction in trial failures. 30% smaller sample sizes. Months saved on protocol development.

#### 3. SafetyNet Engine — Predictive Safety Intelligence

**Problem:** Adverse events are detected too late.

**Solution:**
- **Real-Time Signal Detection**: Continuous analysis of AE reports, lab values, and patient-reported outcomes
- **Predictive Toxicity**: Identifies patients at elevated risk before adverse events occur
- **Causal Inference**: Distinguishes drug-related events from underlying disease progression
- **Automated Narratives**: Generates regulatory-compliant adverse event narratives
- **FDA FAERS Integration**: Cross-references with post-market safety data for early warnings

**Impact:** 3 weeks earlier adverse event detection. 60% reduction in serious AE escalation. 80% faster safety reporting.

#### 4. RegulatoryAI Engine — Autonomous Submission

**Problem:** Regulatory submission is a manual nightmare.

**Solution:**
- **Document Intelligence**: Auto-generates CSRs, IBs, ICFs from trial data
- **Cross-Reference Engine**: Ensures consistency across thousands of submission documents
- **FDA/EMA Compliance Check**: Validates against latest guidance before submission
- **Deficiency Predictor**: Anticipates FDA questions and pre-populates responses
- **Multi-Region Filing**: Simultaneous preparation for FDA, EMA, PMDA, and NMPA submissions

**Impact:** 6 months faster regulatory submission. 50% reduction in deficiency letters. 70% cost savings.

#### 5. SiteCommand Engine — Clinical Operations AI

**Problem:** Site management is chaos.

**Solution:**
- **Site Performance Scoring**: Real-time ranking of sites by enrollment, data quality, and compliance
- **Automated Monitoring**: AI-powered SDV reduces on-site monitoring visits by 60%
- **Query Resolution**: Auto-generates data clarification queries and suggests resolutions
- **Supply Chain Optimization**: Predicts drug supply needs by site and prevents expiration waste
- **Protocol Deviation Detection**: Catches deviations in real-time before they contaminate data

**Impact:** 50% reduction in monitoring costs. 30% improvement in data quality. Real-time site intelligence.

#### 6. DataFusion Engine — Unified Trial Analytics

**Problem:** Trial data is siloed and insights are delayed.

**Solution:**
- **Multi-Modal Integration**: Combines EDC, ePRO, wearables, imaging, genomics into unified patient record
- **Real-Time Dashboards**: Sponsor and site visibility into enrollment, safety, and efficacy metrics
- **Interim Analysis Automation**: Statistically rigorous interim looks without unblinding risk
- **Predictive Trial Outcomes**: Forecasts probability of success with updating confidence intervals
- **Publication-Ready Analytics**: Auto-generates tables, figures, and listings for CSR and publications

**Impact:** 10x faster insights. Unified data platform. Predictive trial intelligence.
