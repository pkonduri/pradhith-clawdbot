## The Technology Moat

### Proprietary Knowledge Extraction Methods

**1. Cognitive Elicitation Framework**
Traditional knowledge capture asks "what do you do?" We've developed AI-driven elicitation that uncovers:
- **Procedural knowledge:** Step-by-step processes
- **Conditional knowledge:** "When X happens, do Y"
- **Contextual knowledge:** Why certain approaches work in certain situations
- **Failure knowledge:** What NOT to do (often the most valuable)
- **Sensory knowledge:** What does a machine "sound like" when something's wrong?

**2. Multi-Modal Knowledge Fusion**
- Text analysis from communications and documents
- Video analysis of expert demonstrations
- Audio analysis of expert explanations
- Sensor data correlation (what were conditions when expert made decision?)

**3. Knowledge Verification Loop**
- AI-generated knowledge is validated by experts
- Disagreements surface deeper, more nuanced knowledge
- Continuous refinement creates increasingly accurate models

**4. Privacy-Preserving Architecture**
- Knowledge stays within enterprise boundaries
- Differential privacy for sensitive expertise
- Role-based access control on knowledge assets
- Audit trails for compliance
