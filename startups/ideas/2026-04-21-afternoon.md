## The Billion-Dollar Insight

**Sales Development is broken.** Every B2B company needs SDRs (Sales Development Representatives) to fill the top of their funnel — prospecting, cold outreach, qualification, meeting booking. But the role is brutally inefficient:

- **$70K+ fully loaded cost** per SDR
- **35% annual turnover** (highest in sales)
- **4-6 months** to full productivity
- **Average SDR tenure: 1.4 years** (they burn out or get promoted)
- **80% of their time** is spent on non-selling activities

**The breakthrough:** AI can now execute the entire SDR workflow autonomously — not just assist, but *replace* — with 10x the output, zero burnout, and continuous improvement.

**The opportunity:** The global sales development market is $100B+. Every B2B company with 50+ employees needs pipeline. We're building the platform that makes human SDRs optional.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. ProspectIQ — Autonomous Target Identification

**What it does:**
- Continuously scans for ideal customer profile (ICP) matches
- Identifies buying signals: job postings, funding rounds, tech stack changes, org changes
- Enriches prospects with 40+ data points from public and proprietary sources
- Prioritizes by likelihood to buy and deal potential

**How it's different:**
- Not just a database lookup — active signal monitoring
- Learns from your closed deals to refine targeting
- Updates in real-time, not quarterly

### 2. MessageForge — Hyper-Personalized Outreach at Scale

**What it does:**
- Generates unique, personalized messages for every prospect
- References specific triggers: recent posts, company news, mutual connections
- Adapts tone and style to match persona preferences
- A/B tests continuously, learning what resonates

**How it's different:**
- Not templates with merge fields — genuinely personal
- Every message is unique — no spam detection triggers
- Learns your brand voice from examples

### 3. ConversationAI — Autonomous Qualification Dialogues

**What it does:**
- Handles multi-turn email and chat conversations
- Asks qualifying questions naturally
- Handles objections with trained responses
- Escalates to humans only when needed

**How it's different:**
- True conversation, not decision trees
- Remembers context across channels and time
- Never gets frustrated, tired, or defensive

### 4. VoiceSDR — AI Phone Conversations (Optional)

**What it does:**
- Makes outbound calls during optimal windows
- Natural voice conversations for qualification
- Handles gatekeepers professionally
- Books meetings in real-time

**How it's different:**
- Indistinguishable from human callers
- Never nervous, always prepared
- Records and transcribes every call

### 5. CalendarSync — Frictionless Meeting Booking

**What it does:**
- Proposes optimal meeting times based on AE availability
- Handles timezone complexity automatically
- Sends confirmations, reminders, and pre-meeting intel
- Reschedules when needed without human intervention

**How it's different:**
- No scheduling ping-pong
- Integrates with all major calendar systems
- Optimizes for AE preferences and meeting density
