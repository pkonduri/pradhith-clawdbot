$500 trillion in assets are locked in illiquid, inaccessible markets. Current tokenization is too expensive, too slow, and too complex.

Prohibitive Costs

$250K-$1M per tokenization. Legal fees, compliance costs, and technical integration make it accessible only to large institutions.
