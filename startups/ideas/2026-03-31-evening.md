## 🎯 The Problem

### Global Trade Has Become Impossible to Navigate

The world trade system is fracturing. In 2026, businesses face:

**1. Tariff Chaos**
- 15,000+ tariff codes across 200+ countries
- Tariff rates changing monthly with geopolitical shifts
- Retaliatory tariff cycles impossible to predict
- Average enterprise loses $2.3M annually to tariff misclassification

**2. Rules of Origin Nightmare**
- Complex calculations determine tariff eligibility
- Free trade agreements have conflicting requirements
- One wrong component can void preferential treatment
- Compliance requires tracking every input's origin

**3. Supply Chain Tariff Exposure**
- Most companies don't know their true tariff costs
- Hidden tariffs buried in supplier pricing
- Multi-tier supply chains create blind spots
- Restructuring decisions made with incomplete data

**4. Regulatory Whiplash**
- New trade regulations weekly
- Section 301, 232, anti-dumping duties constantly shifting
- Export controls expanding rapidly
- Sanctions compliance increasingly complex

**The Current "Solutions" Are Broken:**
- Customs brokers: Reactive, expensive, no strategic insight
- Trade compliance software: Data entry tools, not intelligence
- Consultants: Slow, expensive, project-based
- Manual research: Impossible to keep current

**The $847B Question:**
U.S. companies alone paid $847B in tariffs over the past 5 years. How much of that was avoidable?
