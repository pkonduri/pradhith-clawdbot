## The Problem

### The Investigation Crisis in the AI Age

**1. Synthetic Content Explosion**

- AI-generated text, images, audio, and video are flooding every platform
- Deepfakes can fabricate evidence or alibis with near-perfect fidelity
- Traditional forensic tools can't distinguish synthetic from authentic content
- GitHub bots, review spam, social media manipulation at unprecedented scale

**2. Data Volume Overwhelm**

- Average corporate investigation involves 5TB+ of data
- Law enforcement cases can span dozens of devices, platforms, and jurisdictions
- Human investigators can only review 50-100 documents per day
- 95% of potentially relevant evidence is never examined

**3. Fragmented Tooling**

- Digital forensics: Cellebrite, FTK, EnCase (separate tools)
- eDiscovery: Relativity, Disco, Everlaw (another stack)
- OSINT: Manual processes, cobbled-together scripts
- No unified intelligence layer connecting these workflows

**4. Chain of Custody Challenges**

- AI-assisted evidence handling creates authentication questions
- Courts increasingly scrutinizing digital evidence integrity
- No standard for AI-involved forensic processes
- Admissibility at risk without proper documentation
