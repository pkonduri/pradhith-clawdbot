The Crisis

Centralized Manufacturing is Collapsing

The global manufacturing system was built for a world that no longer exists. Five forces are shattering the old playbook simultaneously.

💰

Tariff Wars

US-China tariffs at 60-145%, EU imposing carbon border taxes. The era of frictionless global trade is over.

$2.3T trade flows disrupted
⛓️

Supply Chain Fragility

COVID, Suez blockage, Red Sea attacks exposed fatal single-point failures in concentrated supply chains.

$4T in losses 2020-2025
👷

Labor Cost Convergence

Chinese wages up 300% since 2010. Automation is eliminating the labor arbitrage that drove offshoring.

15% gap narrowing per year
🌍

Carbon Regulations

Scope 3 emissions now mandatory. Shipping under heavy scrutiny. Green manufacturing isn't optional anymore.

$850B compliance costs coming
⚡

Speed-to-Market Pressure

12-week ocean shipping is incompatible with fast fashion, customization, and agile product cycles.

67% cite as critical issue
🏛️

Reshoring Mandates

CHIPS Act, EU Critical Raw Materials Act, defense requirements. Governments are forcing production home.

$400B in global incentives
