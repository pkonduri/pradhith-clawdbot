# TierZero AI — The Autonomous IT Support Platform

> **One-liner:** AI agents that resolve 85% of IT tickets before a human ever sees them.

---

## The Problem

Enterprise IT support is broken:

- **$200B+ spent annually** on IT support services globally
- **Average ticket resolution:** 24+ hours for Tier 1, days for Tier 2
- **Employee productivity loss:** 22 minutes average per IT issue (while waiting for help)
- **IT staff burnout:** 70% of IT support time spent on repetitive, low-complexity tickets
- **Talent shortage:** 3.5M unfilled cybersecurity/IT positions globally
- **Shadow IT explosion:** Frustrated employees bypass IT, creating security risks

The current approach—human agents triaging tickets through outdated ITSM platforms—cannot scale. Every company with 500+ employees is drowning in password resets, access requests, VPN issues, and "my laptop is slow" tickets.

**The AI agent revolution changes everything.**

---

## The Solution: TierZero AI

TierZero AI is the **autonomous IT support platform** that handles tickets from initial request through resolution—no human required for 85% of issues.
