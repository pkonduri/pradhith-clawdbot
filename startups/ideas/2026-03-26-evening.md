# WildfireOS — Autonomous Wildfire Prevention & Response Intelligence

## The Billion-Dollar Thesis

**One sentence:** WildfireOS is the AI operating system that predicts, prevents, and coordinates wildfire response using satellite imagery, IoT sensors, weather data, and multi-agency orchestration.

**The Setup:** Wildfires now cause $100B+ in annual damages globally. Climate change has extended fire seasons by 78 days. Insurance carriers are fleeing high-risk states. Utilities face billions in liability. Yet firefighting agencies still rely on 1990s-era coordination tools and human spotters.

**The Gap:** No unified AI platform exists that can predict ignition risk at the property level, detect fires within seconds of ignition, and autonomously coordinate multi-agency response while optimizing resource deployment in real-time.

**The Vision:** Become the Palantir for wildfire—the intelligence layer that governments, utilities, and insurers cannot operate without.

### Layer 1: Omniscient Sensing

```

│ • Private SAR   │ • Wind towers   │ • Social media signals  │
└─────────────────┴─────────────────┴─────────────────────────┘
```

**Capabilities:**
- Sub-minute fire detection (vs. 15+ minutes current standard)
- 10-meter resolution risk mapping updated hourly
- Real-time wind modeling for fire spread prediction
- Fuel load assessment via multispectral analysis

### Layer 2: Predictive Intelligence

**FireMind™ AI Engine:**
- **Ignition Risk Scoring:** Property-level probability of fire start (0-100)
- **Spread Modeling:** ML-based fire behavior prediction (24-72 hour forecasts)
- **Resource Optimization:** Optimal pre-positioning of crews and equipment
- **Evacuation Planning:** Dynamic routing based on fire progression

**Core Models:**
- Transformer-based satellite imagery analysis (fine-tuned on 500K+ historical fires)
- Physics-informed neural networks for fire spread dynamics
- Graph neural networks for power line failure prediction
- Reinforcement learning for resource allocation optimization

### Layer 3: Command & Coordination

**AgentSwarm™ Response System:**
- Autonomous dispatch recommendations to ICS (Incident Command System)
- Multi-agency coordination (CAL FIRE, USFS, local departments)
- Real-time resource tracking and optimization
- Automated reporting and compliance documentation

**Interface Options:**
- Mobile app for field commanders
- Web dashboard for operations centers
- API for insurance/utility integration
- AR overlay for aircraft operations
