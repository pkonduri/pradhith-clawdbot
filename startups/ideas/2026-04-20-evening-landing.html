The Solution

Autonomous intelligence for ecosystem restoration at planetary scale.

01

Intelligent Site Assessment

Before any restoration begins, RegenOS analyzes historical ecosystem state, current soil health, hydrology, connectivity, climate trajectory, and community needs to produce probabilistic restoration roadmaps.

02

Species Selection Engine

AI selects optimal species mixes based on native templates, climate-resilient genetics, biodiversity value, carbon potential, and economic value for communities. No monocultures—ever.

03

Multi-Sensor Monitoring Fusion

Satellite imagery, drone surveys, ground sensors, eDNA sampling, acoustic monitoring, and camera traps—all feeding digital twins that model ecosystem health trajectories in real-time.

04

Automated MRV & Credits

Biomass estimation, soil carbon analysis, biodiversity indices, additionality proof, permanence risk scoring, and audit-ready reporting for Verra, Gold Standard, and ART.

05

Indigenous Knowledge Integration

Built with indigenous communities, not around them. Traditional ecological knowledge databases, community protocols, revenue sharing, and local steward networks.
