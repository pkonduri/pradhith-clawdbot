## 🎯 The Thesis

**Centralized identity verification is a ticking time bomb.**

Yesterday's news: IDMerit—a KYC provider trusted by banks and fintech companies—left 1 billion sensitive records exposed on the open internet. Names, addresses, dates of birth, Social Security numbers, national IDs. Everything criminals need to steal your identity, sitting in an unprotected MongoDB database.

This isn't an anomaly. It's the inevitable failure mode of the current KYC architecture.

**Every company that verifies identity today becomes a honeypot.** They're legally required to collect your most sensitive data, and they store it in databases that become high-value targets. When (not if) those databases are breached, the damage is catastrophic and irreversible.

The solution isn't better database security. **The solution is zero-storage verification.**

---

## 💡 The Product

### ProofID: Verify Everything. Store Nothing.

ProofID is an identity verification platform built on a revolutionary principle: **cryptographic proof without data possession.**

Using zero-knowledge proofs and homomorphic encryption, businesses can verify that you are who you say you are—without ever receiving, storing, or possessing your raw identity data.

**Even if ProofID's servers are completely compromised, there's nothing to steal.** No names. No addresses. No SSNs. No documents. Just cryptographic proofs that are mathematically useless to attackers.

### Zero-Knowledge Proofs for Identity

**Example: Age Verification**
- Traditional KYC: Business receives your driver's license, stores your DOB, calculates your age
- ProofID: Your device generates a ZK proof that mathematically demonstrates "DOB < March 12, 2005" without revealing your actual DOB

**Example: Income Verification**
- Traditional KYC: Business receives bank statements, stores your transaction history
- ProofID: Your device generates a ZK proof from encrypted bank data that proves "annual income > $75,000" without revealing exact amounts or transactions

**Example: Watchlist Screening**
- Traditional KYC: Business sends your name to screening service, receives and stores results
- ProofID: ZK proof demonstrates "user does not match any entry in OFAC/PEP lists" without revealing your name to anyone
