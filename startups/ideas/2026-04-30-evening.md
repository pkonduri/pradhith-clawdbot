## How It Works

### 1. Discovery Engine — AI-Designed Antimicrobials

**The Problem:** Traditional drug discovery takes 10-15 years and $2B+ per drug. Most antibiotics fail in trials.

**Our Solution:**

- **Generative Molecular AI**: Design novel antimicrobial compounds from first principles
- **Mechanism Prediction**: Target bacterial processes with zero human homologues (minimizing side effects)
- **Resistance Forecasting**: Predict how bacteria will evolve resistance before synthesis
- **Multi-Target Design**: Create compounds that attack multiple bacterial pathways simultaneously
- **Natural Product Mining**: AI analysis of untapped microbial biodiversity (soil, ocean, extreme environments)

**Key Innovation:** Our "Resistance-First Design" methodology predicts evolutionary escape routes and blocks them proactively.

### 2. Surveillance Network — Global Resistance Intelligence

**The Problem:** Resistance emerges locally, spreads globally. Current tracking is fragmented and delayed by months.

**Our Solution:**

- **Real-Time Genomic Surveillance**: Integration with hospital lab systems, wastewater monitoring, agricultural testing
- **Resistance Gene Tracking**: Identify and trace resistance genes (plasmids, cassettes) across geographies
- **Outbreak Prediction**: ML models predict where resistance will emerge next (7-30 day forecasts)
- **Cross-Border Intelligence**: Track resistance patterns across food supply chains, travel corridors, migration routes
- **Sentinel Network**: AI-powered early warning from partner labs globally

**Data Sources:**
- Hospital EHR/LIMS integrations
- Wastewater genomic surveillance
- Agricultural inspection data
- Travel and trade flow data
- Environmental sampling networks
- Published and preprint literature

### 3. Stewardship AI — Optimizing Every Prescription

**The Problem:** 30-50% of antibiotic prescriptions are unnecessary or inappropriate, accelerating resistance.

**Our Solution:**

**For Hospitals:**
- **Real-Time Prescribing Guidance**: AI recommends optimal antibiotic, dose, duration based on patient factors and local resistance patterns
- **De-Escalation Alerts**: Prompt narrowing of therapy when culture results return
- **Antibiogram Intelligence**: Continuously updated local resistance data
- **Outbreak Detection**: Identify clusters before they spread
- **Stewardship Dashboards**: Track metrics, benchmark against peers

**For Clinics/Outpatient:**
- **Point-of-Care Decision Support**: Differentiate viral vs. bacterial at bedside
- **Delayed Prescribing Protocols**: Smart systems for watchful waiting
- **Patient Education**: AI-powered communication to reduce demand for unnecessary antibiotics

**For Agriculture:**
- **Prophylaxis Optimization**: Reduce routine antibiotic use while maintaining animal health
- **Feed Additive Intelligence**: Alternative antimicrobials (bacteriophages, probiotics, etc.)
- **Resistance Monitoring**: Track farm-level and supply chain patterns

### 4. Economic Engine — Sustainable Antibiotic Innovation

**The Problem:** Antibiotics are economically broken. Companies can't profit because we WANT them used sparingly.

**Our Solution:**

- **Subscription Model Infrastructure**: Build the rails for hospital/government subscription payments for antibiotic availability (not volume)
- **Pull Incentive Coordination**: Aggregate demand signals for new antibiotics to de-risk R&D
- **Value-Based Contracting**: Price antibiotics based on resistance-avoided, not doses sold
- **Portfolio Intelligence**: Help developers identify highest-value targets based on unmet resistance needs
- **Regulatory Pathway Optimization**: AI-assisted trial design to accelerate approvals

### Resistance-First Drug Design Methodology

1. **Target Identification**
   - Identify essential bacterial genes with low mutational tolerance
   - Prioritize multi-copy targets (harder to evolve resistance)
   - Avoid targets with known bypass mechanisms

2. **Escape Route Mapping**
   - Simulate all possible resistance mutations computationally
   - Predict fitness costs of each resistance path
   - Identify evolutionary dead-ends

3. **Multi-Hit Design**
   - Design compounds that require multiple simultaneous mutations for resistance
   - Target conserved sites under negative selection
   - Create compounds with multiple binding modes

4. **Resistance Surveillance Integration**
   - Continuously update models with emerging resistance
   - Prioritize compounds effective against latest strains
   - Deprecate compounds as resistance spreads
