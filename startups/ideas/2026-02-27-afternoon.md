## Executive Summary

**SafetyOS** is the AI-native operating system that eliminates workplace injuries and deaths. By fusing real-time sensor data, computer vision, predictive analytics, and behavioral AI, SafetyOS creates a "digital safety officer" that monitors every corner of industrial operations 24/7—preventing accidents before they happen.

**The Problem:** 2.3 million workers die from workplace accidents annually. U.S. companies pay $170B/year in workers' compensation. Despite decades of safety programs, incident rates have plateaued—human vigilance has limits.

**The Solution:** An autonomous safety platform that detects hazards in real-time (falling objects, toxic gas, equipment failures, unsafe behaviors), predicts incidents before they occur, automates compliance reporting, and continuously learns from near-misses across industries.

**Business Model:** SaaS platform ($50K-500K/year per facility) + sensor hardware ($10K-100K per deployment) + insurance premium reduction sharing.

**Market:** $82B TAM by 2028 (industrial safety technology). Every factory, construction site, warehouse, oil rig, and mine is a customer.

**Traction Goal:** 500 facilities monitored by Month 18. 50% incident reduction demonstrated in pilots by Month 6.

The fundamental problem: **human vigilance cannot scale.**

### Core Capabilities

#### 1. Real-Time Hazard Detection
- **Computer Vision:** Detects PPE violations, unsafe postures, proximity to machinery, falling objects, spills, fire hazards
- **Environmental Sensors:** Gas leaks, temperature extremes, noise levels, air quality, radiation
- **Equipment Monitoring:** Vibration anomalies, wear patterns, failure predictions
- **Worker Biometrics:** Fatigue detection, heat stress, respiratory rate (opt-in wearables)

#### 2. Predictive Incident Prevention
- **Risk Scoring:** Every zone, shift, and activity gets a real-time risk score
- **Pattern Recognition:** Identifies combinations of factors that precede accidents
- **Early Warning System:** Alerts supervisors 15-60 minutes before predicted incidents
- **Weather Integration:** Adjusts outdoor work risk based on conditions

#### 3. Autonomous Compliance
- **Auto-Documentation:** Every safety observation logged automatically
- **Inspection Prep:** Pre-generates OSHA inspection reports
- **Training Triggers:** Identifies workers needing refresher training
- **Audit Trail:** Immutable record for liability protection

#### 4. Federated Learning Network
- **Cross-Industry Intelligence:** Learn from near-misses across thousands of facilities
- **Privacy-Preserving:** Models improve without sharing raw data
- **Continuous Improvement:** Each deployment makes the entire network smarter
