## The Problem

### The Battery Industry is Scaling Chaos

**1. Manufacturing Quality is the Make-or-Break Challenge**
- Battery cells have 100+ process steps with microscopic tolerances
- Defect rates of 5-15% vs. <1% in mature semiconductors
- A single dendrite can cause thermal runaway and fires
- EV recalls from battery defects cost $1B+ (see: GM Bolt, Hyundai Kona)

**2. Yield Optimization is a Black Box**
- Cell formation takes 2-3 weeks per batch
- Feedback loops are impossibly slow for iterative improvement
- Engineers correlate thousands of variables manually
- No standardized approach to process-quality correlation

**3. Supply Chain is a Geopolitical Minefield**
- 70% of lithium processing in China
- 60% of cobalt from DRC with child labor concerns
- Nickel supply disrupted by Indonesia export restrictions
- Battery-grade graphite concentrated in China
- Single-source dependencies across critical materials

**4. End-of-Life is an Environmental Time Bomb**
- <5% of lithium-ion batteries are recycled globally
- 11 million tons of spent batteries projected by 2030
- Second-life value capture is minimal
- Regulatory pressure mounting (EU Battery Passport mandate)

**5. Safety and Compliance Complexity**
- Different requirements across US, EU, China, Korea
- UN38.3 transportation testing alone takes months
- Battery passport requirements creating data infrastructure needs
- Insurance underwriting requires extensive quality documentation
