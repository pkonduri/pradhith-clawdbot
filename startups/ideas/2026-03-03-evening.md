## рҹҡҖ The Solution: SkyGrid AI

### The Four Pillars

**1. Real-Time Deconfliction Engine**
Our ML models process millions of flight paths simultaneously, predicting conflicts 30+ minutes ahead and generating optimal re-routing in milliseconds. Think high-frequency trading algorithms applied to airspace management.

**2. Dynamic Airspace Intelligence**
We integrate 47 real-time data feeds: weather (micro-level), emergency services, temporary flight restrictions, bird migration patterns, noise ordinances, and more. The airspace isn't static вҖ” our intelligence layer adapts in real-time.

**3. Universal Flight API**
One API to rule them all. Operators submit flight plans, receive optimized routes, get real-time deconfliction, and maintain compliance вҖ” regardless of their hardware, mission type, or operating region.

**4. Regulatory Compliance Engine**
We've encoded FAA Part 107, Part 135, and emerging Part 108 regulations into our system. Operators get automatic compliance verification, audit trails, and regulatory reporting. When rules change, we update once вҖ” all operators benefit immediately.
