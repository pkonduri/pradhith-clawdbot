## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Regulatory Risk: "Unauthorized Practice of Law"

**Risk:** State bar associations may claim AI immigration assistance is UPL
**Mitigation:**
- Every case reviewed by licensed attorney
- Clearly separate "information" from "advice"
- Partner with law firms for representation
- Active engagement with bar associations
- Precedent: LegalZoom, Rocket Lawyer successfully navigated

### Policy Volatility Risk

**Risk:** Immigration policy changes dramatically (new administration)
**Mitigation:**
- Real-time policy intelligence is our core competency
- Changes create confusion → drive demand for our service
- Multi-country platform hedges against single-country risk
- Enterprise contracts have policy-change provisions

### Competition Risk

**Risk:** Fragomen or Envoy builds AI capabilities
**Mitigation:**
- Legacy tech debt slows incumbents
- AI-native architecture advantage
- Consumer brand protects against pure enterprise play
- Data network effects compound over time

### Quality Risk
