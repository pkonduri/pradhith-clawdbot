### The SMB Finance Crisis

Small and medium businesses are flying blind financially:

**1. The Expertise Gap**

| Company Size | Access to Financial Strategy |
|-------------|------------------------------|
| Fortune 500 | Full CFO + finance team + consultants |
| Mid-Market ($50M+) | Part-time CFO or controller |

- Most SMBs only know they're in trouble when it's too late
- Cash flow crises appear "suddenly" (but were predictable)
- Accountants provide historical data, not future insight
- By the time quarterly reports arrive, opportunities are gone

**3. The Tool Overload Paradox**
- Average SMB uses 7-10 financial tools
- QuickBooks, Excel, banking apps, expense tools, payroll systems
- **None of them talk to each other intelligently**
- Owner spends 15+ hours/week on financial admin

**4. The Decision Vacuum**
- "Should I take this loan?" — No one to ask
- "Can I afford to hire?" — Guess and hope
- "What price should I charge?" — Competitor copycatting
- "When do I run out of cash?" — Surprise!
