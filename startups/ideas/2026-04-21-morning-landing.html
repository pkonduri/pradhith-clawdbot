The Problem

Flying Blind to Biological Reality

Every critical sector lacks real-time visibility into the biological environment. The consequences are catastrophic.

🏥

Pandemic Blindness

Hospital-based surveillance detects outbreaks 2-3 weeks after community spread begins. By then, exponential growth is unstoppable.

🦎

Conservation Guesswork

Wildlife surveys cost $50K+, take months, and miss 90% of species. We're losing biodiversity faster than we can measure it.

🌾

Crop Destruction

$40B in US crops lost annually to pests and disease. Detection happens after visible damage—when it's already too late.

🏙️

Silent Cities

Smart city sensors measure air quality, traffic, noise—but completely miss the biological dimension of urban health.

🏢

Sick Buildings

Mold, pathogens, and allergens go undetected until occupants get sick. Annual air tests are point-in-time snapshots—easily gamed.

☣️

Bio Threats Unmonitored

No ambient monitoring for biological threats. Detection requires direct sampling of suspicious materials—post-event only.
