### Core Features

#### 1. **Intelligent Delegation Framework**
Define your delegation parameters:
- **Listen Mode:** AI attends silently, extracts relevant information
- **Consult Mode:** AI asks clarifying questions, gathers context
- **Decide Mode:** AI makes decisions within pre-defined boundaries
- **Commit Mode:** AI can commit resources, timelines, or approvals

#### 2. **Contextual Intelligence**
- Ingests your emails, documents, past meeting history
- Understands your opinions, preferences, and decision patterns
- Knows your relationships with other attendees
- Tracks commitments made in previous meetings

#### 3. **Real-time Participation**
- Natural voice presence in Zoom, Teams, Meet, and any conferencing platform
- Responds to direct questions with context-appropriate answers
- Asks clarifying questions when needed
- Can request to escalate to you in real-time for critical decisions

#### 4. **Post-Meeting Intelligence**
- Structured summary with decisions, action items, and commitments
- Highlights anything that deviated from your parameters
- Suggests follow-up actions
- Updates your calendar and task management systems
