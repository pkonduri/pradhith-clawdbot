## 🎯 Problem Deep Dive

### Why Travel is Broken

1. **Modal Silos**
   - Airlines don't talk to Amtrak
   - Amtrak doesn't integrate with local transit
   - Uber/Lyft are afterthoughts
   - Each mode optimizes for itself, not the traveler

2. **Real-Time Blindness**
   - Book a flight at 6 AM, it's delayed by 8 AM
   - No system tells you "take the 5:30 train instead"
   - Travelers discover problems after they're trapped

3. **Carbon Calculus Chaos**
   - 67% of business travelers want lower-carbon options
   - 0% of tools make this easy to compare
   - "Green" travel means sacrifice, not optimization

4. **The Last-Mile Disaster**
   - Airport → Hotel coordination is manual
   - Missed connections cascade into chaos
   - No unified view of door-to-door journey

### The Airport Meltdown

Recent events have exposed critical vulnerabilities:
- TSA staffing shortages creating 3-hour security lines
- Aging infrastructure unable to handle volume
- Weather disruptions with no alternative routing
- Corporate travelers abandoning flights for trains

**This is a secular shift, not a blip.** Rail ridership is up 34% YoY. Amtrak just reported record bookings. Europe's train renaissance is coming to America.
