#### 2. Onboarding & Training
- Standardized agent deployment templates

- Knowledge base provisioning

#### 3. Performance Management
- Real-time quality scoring
- Hallucination rate tracking
- Task completion metrics
- Cost-per-outcome analysis
- Automated regression detection

#### 4. Governance & Compliance
- Role-based access control (RBAC) for AI
- Audit trails for every agent action
- PII handling policies

- Kill switches and containment protocols

#### 5. Coordination Hub
- Human-AI workflow orchestration
- Agent-to-agent communication protocols
- Escalation rules (when does AI hand off to human?)

- Real-time spend tracking by agent
- Budget alerts and caps
