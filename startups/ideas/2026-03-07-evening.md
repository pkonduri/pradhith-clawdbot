## The Billion-Dollar Problem

**Cyber breaches are inevitable. The response is still medieval.**

The average data breach costs **$4.88 million** in 2025. Companies take **277 days** on average to identify and contain a breach. During that time, attackers exfiltrate data, establish persistence, and the damage compounds exponentially.

Current incident response is chaos:
- **Manual triage** — security teams drowning in alerts, missing real threats
- **Fragmented tools** — SIEM, SOAR, EDR, XDR — none talk to each other well
- **Forensics bottleneck** — expensive consultants, weeks to understand what happened
- **Communication breakdown** — legal, PR, executives, regulators all need different info
- **Compliance nightmare** — GDPR requires 72-hour notification, HIPAA, PCI, SEC rules...

The incident response market is **$33 billion** and growing 20%+ annually. Yet most companies still rely on playbooks, manual runbooks, and hoping their MSP picks up the phone at 3 AM.
