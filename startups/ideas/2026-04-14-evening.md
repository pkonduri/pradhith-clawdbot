# GeneShield AI

## The Privacy & Sovereignty Platform for Genetic Data

**One-liner:** The Plaid for genetic data — giving individuals control, privacy, and economic rights over their most permanent identifier.

## The Problem

### Your DNA Is Forever — And It's Already Out There

**The Genetic Data Crisis:**
- **100M+ people** have shared DNA with consumer testing companies
- **23andMe's bankruptcy** exposed the vulnerability of genetic databases
- **Data breaches** have leaked millions of genetic profiles
- **AI can now infer** health conditions, ancestry, family relationships, and traits from minimal genetic data
- **Once leaked, genetic data cannot be changed** — unlike passwords or credit cards

**The Stakes Are Enormous:**
- **Insurance discrimination:** Life, health, disability insurers increasingly want genetic data
- **Employment risks:** Genetic predispositions could affect hiring decisions
- **Family exposure:** Your DNA reveals information about relatives who never consented
- **Law enforcement overreach:** Genetic genealogy databases used without proper warrants
- **Future risks unknown:** What will AI be able to infer from DNA in 10 years?

**The Market Failure:**
- Consumer testing companies treated genetic data as an asset to monetize
- No infrastructure exists for individuals to control their genetic information
- GINA (Genetic Information Nondiscrimination Act) has major gaps
- International genetic data flows have zero governance
- Individuals have no way to know who has their genetic data or how it's being used

#### 3. Rights Management Engine
- **Consent management** across all parties who've accessed your genetic data
- **Deletion requests** automated to GDPR/CCPA standards and beyond
- **Licensing framework** for compensated genetic data sharing

#### 4. Privacy-Preserving Sharing
- **Selective attribute disclosure** — share "low heart disease risk" without genome
- **Federated learning participation** — contribute to research without exposing data
- **Encrypted matching** — find relatives or donors without revealing identities
- **Temporary access tokens** — time-limited data access for specific purposes
- **Audit trails** — immutable logs of all data access and usage

#### 5. AI-Powered Protection
- **Inference attack detection** — identify attempts to reconstruct your genome
- **Synthetic data generation** — create decoy profiles for honeypot monitoring
- **Risk scoring** — understand your genetic privacy exposure level

### Key Technical Innovations

**1. Genetic Data Sharding:**
- Split genetic data across multiple jurisdictions
- No single point of compromise
- Reconstruct only with user's cryptographic keys
- Automatic re-sharding on threat detection

**2. Zero-Knowledge Genetic Proofs:**
- Prove genetic attributes without revealing genome
- "I have low Alzheimer's risk" without showing APOE status
- Cryptographic verification of claims
- Selective disclosure at attribute level

**3. Inference Attack Detection:**
- AI monitors for patterns suggesting reconstruction attempts
- Detects when partial data requests could enable re-identification
- Automatic protective measures (noise injection, access throttling)
- Alert user to potential compromise

**4. Synthetic Decoy Generation:**
- Create realistic but fake genetic profiles
- Deploy as honeypots across dark web
- Detect when your data appears in breaches
- Misdirect malicious actors
