### The Nine Core Agents

#### 1. CustomerBot — 24/7 Customer Communication
- Answers calls, texts, and messages anytime
- Schedules appointments, answers FAQs, takes orders
- Escalates complex issues to owner via push notification
- Learns your business voice and policies

**Real Example**: A plumber's CustomerBot answers "We have a leak!" at 2 AM, asks qualifying questions, schedules an emergency visit, and texts the plumber with details — all before they wake up.

#### 2. ScheduleBot — Intelligent Scheduling
- AI-powered appointment booking
- Optimizes routes for service businesses
- Handles cancellations, reschedules, reminders
- Syncs with Google Calendar, iCal, any system

**Real Example**: A dog groomer's ScheduleBot automatically fills a cancellation gap by texting clients on the waitlist, confirming the best fit, and updating the calendar.

#### 3. ResponseBot — Review & Reputation Management
- Monitors Google, Yelp, Facebook for reviews
- Drafts personalized responses (owner approves in one tap)
- Alerts on negative reviews instantly
- Generates review requests to happy customers

**Real Example**: A restaurant owner gets notified of a 2-star review, sees a drafted apologetic response, edits one word, and posts — all in 30 seconds on their phone.

#### 4. FollowUpBot — Automated Customer Nurturing
- Post-service follow-up messages
- Re-engagement campaigns for dormant customers
- Birthday/anniversary messages
- Quote/estimate follow-ups

**Real Example**: An auto repair shop's FollowUpBot sends "Time for an oil change!" reminders at exactly the right mileage interval, personalized to each customer.

#### 5. InvoiceBot — Smart Billing & Collections
- Generates and sends invoices automatically
- Friendly payment reminders (escalating in tone)
- Accepts payments via text
- Syncs with QuickBooks, Wave, FreshBooks

**Real Example**: A freelance photographer's InvoiceBot sends an invoice immediately after a shoot, follows up at 7, 14, and 30 days, and enables one-tap payment.

#### 6. InsightBot — Business Intelligence
- Daily/weekly business health reports
- "What's working" analysis (which services, times, customers)
- Trend alerts ("Bookings down 20% vs. last month")
- Simple, actionable recommendations

**Real Example**: A yoga studio owner gets a Sunday night text: "Great week! Revenue up 15%. Thursday 6PM class at capacity 3 weeks straight — consider adding a second?"

#### 7. MarketBot — Local Marketing Automation
- Google Business Profile optimization
- Social media posting (owner approves content)
- Local SEO monitoring
- Seasonal campaign suggestions

**Real Example**: MarketBot notices competitor ranking higher for "emergency electrician [city]" and suggests content/review strategies to improve ranking.

#### 8. ComplianceBot — Regulatory Assistance
- License renewal reminders
- Industry-specific compliance checklists
- Document organization
- OSHA, HIPAA, food safety guidance (by industry)

**Real Example**: A food truck operator gets reminded 30 days before health permit renewal, with a checklist of what's needed and pre-filled forms.

#### 9. OperationsBot — Day-to-Day Orchestration
- Inventory alerts for product businesses
- Equipment maintenance reminders
- Employee scheduling assistance
- Vendor coordination

**Real Example**: A coffee shop's OperationsBot notices milk deliveries have been inconsistent and suggests switching vendors, including quotes from alternatives.
