**The pharmaceutical industry is fundamentally broken.**

- **$2.6B average cost** to develop a single new drug

- mRNA and peptide therapies can be customized rapidly

**The missing piece:** A unified platform that connects AI drug design → personalized formulation → on-demand manufacturing → patient delivery.

**PharmGen AI is that platform.**

## Regulatory Strategy

### FDA Pathway

**Current regulatory assets:**
1. **21st Century Cures Act**: Expedited pathways for personalized medicine
2. **FDA Guidance on Individualized Antisense Oligonucleotides**: Precedent for N=1 drugs
3. **503B Compounding**: Allows personalized formulations under physician supervision
4. **Breakthrough Therapy Designation**: Fast-track for serious conditions
5. **Right to Try Act**: Access for terminal patients

**Our approach:**
- Start in 503B compounding (established regulatory framework)
- Build evidence for FDA pre-submission meetings
- Pursue breakthrough designation for oncology applications
- Work with FDA on adaptive regulatory framework for AI-designed drugs

### Compliance Infrastructure
- Real-time audit trails
- Explainable AI for every drug design
- cGMP manufacturing standards
- HIPAA-compliant patient data handling
- SOC 2 Type II certification
