├── Buyer places order (limit or market)
├── Order book matches buyer to seller
├── Smart contract locks payment in escrow
└── Settlement process initiated

STEP 3: SETTLEMENT
├── Buyer generates fresh key pair

├── Buyer receives model, verifies integrity
├── Smart contract releases payment to seller
└── Ownership transferred on-chain
