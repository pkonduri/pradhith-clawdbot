The Problem

The Battery Industry is Scaling Chaos

$500B in investments, but manufacturers are drowning in quality issues, supply chain risks, and regulatory complexity.

💥

Quality Crisis

Battery defect rates of 5-15% vs. <1% in semiconductors. EV recalls from battery issues have cost the industry billions.

$5B+ in recall costs
🌍

Supply Chain Risk

70% of lithium processing in China. 60% of cobalt from DRC. Single-source dependencies across critical materials.

6-month lead times
⏱️

Slow Feedback Loops

Cell formation takes 2-3 weeks per batch. Engineers can't iterate fast enough to optimize yield and quality.

100+ process steps
♻️

End-of-Life Crisis

Less than 5% of lithium-ion batteries are recycled globally. 11M tons of spent batteries projected by 2030.

Environmental time bomb
📋

Regulatory Pressure

EU Battery Passport mandatory from 2027. Different requirements across US, EU, China, Korea creating compliance chaos.

Multi-jurisdiction maze
🔥

Safety Concerns

A single dendrite can cause thermal runaway and fires. Prediction and prevention require correlating millions of data points.

Brand-destroying risk
