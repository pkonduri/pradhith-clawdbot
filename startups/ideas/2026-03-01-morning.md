**One-liner:** The operating system that turns food waste from a $1.2 trillion global crisis into a multi-billion dollar optimization opportunity.

---

## 🚨 The Problem

Food waste is one of humanity's most solvable yet most ignored catastrophes:

- **$1.2 trillion** in food wasted globally every year
- **40% of all food** produced in the US is thrown away
- Food waste generates **8-10% of global greenhouse gas emissions** — more than the airline industry
- Restaurants lose **4-10% of food purchased** before it reaches customers
- Grocery stores discard **$15.6 billion** in unsold food annually
- 30% of farm produce **never leaves the farm** due to cosmetic standards

Meanwhile:
- **44 million Americans** face food insecurity
- Food banks receive only **a fraction** of what they need
- Composting and diversion infrastructure is fragmented and manual

**The brutal truth:** The food supply chain has no unified intelligence layer. Every stage — farms, processors, distributors, retailers, restaurants, consumers — operates in silos with primitive demand forecasting, zero real-time coordination, and archaic expiration management.

---

## 💡 The Solution: WasteZero AI

**WasteZero AI is the autonomous intelligence layer that eliminates food waste across the entire supply chain.**

We deploy AI agents at every node of the food system to:

1. **Predict demand with 95%+ accuracy** using hyperlocal signals (weather, events, social trends, economic indicators)
2. **Optimize inventory in real-time** to minimize over-ordering and spoilage
3. **Dynamically reroute surplus** to secondary markets, food banks, composters, and biogas facilities
4. **Extend shelf life** through smart pricing, automated markdowns, and optimal storage recommendations
5. **Create perfect matching** between surplus food and demand (B2B, B2C, donation, and processing)
