## 🎯 Problem Deep Dive

### Why Traffic is Still Broken in 2026

1. **Legacy Signal Systems**
   - 95% of US traffic signals use fixed-timing or basic adaptive algorithms
   - Average signal infrastructure is 25+ years old
   - Systems don't communicate across jurisdictions
   - Zero integration with real-time vehicle data

2. **The Coordination Failure**
   - No entity optimizes city-wide traffic as a system
   - Google Maps optimizes for individual drivers (creates new congestion)
   - Waze routing recommendations cause neighborhood chaos
   - Delivery companies, transit, and personal vehicles don't coordinate

3. **Data Fragmentation**
   - DOTs have sensor data but no AI
   - AVs have intelligence but no city integration
   - Mapping companies have routes but no signal control
   - Emergency services operate in silos

4. **Political Gridlock**
   - Multiple agencies own different roads
   - No unified traffic authority in most metros
   - Privacy concerns block data sharing
   - Infrastructure funding is siloed
