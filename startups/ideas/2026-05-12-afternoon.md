# AgentAudit — The SOC 2 for AI Agents

## The Vision
**AgentAudit is the enterprise compliance and certification platform that audits, certifies, and continuously monitors AI agent behavior — becoming the trusted standard for AI agent deployment in regulated industries.**

Every enterprise wants to deploy AI agents. None of them can prove to their board, auditors, regulators, or customers that those agents are safe, compliant, and behaving as intended. AgentAudit closes that trust gap.

---

## The Problem

### The AI Agent Deployment Paradox
- **$150B+ enterprise AI market** by 2027 — growing 37% annually
- **87% of enterprises** say autonomous AI agents are "critical" to their 2027 strategy
- **Only 12%** have deployed production AI agents at scale
- **#1 blocker:** "We can't prove to regulators and auditors that agents are safe"

### The Compliance Nightmare

| Challenge | Enterprise Reality | Cost |
|-----------|-------------------|------|
| **No audit trail** | AI agents make thousands of decisions — no consistent logging standard | Failed audits, regulatory fines |
| **Behavior drift** | Agent behavior changes over time with model updates | Compliance violations discovered months later |
| **Shadow agents** | Employees deploy unapproved AI tools | Data breaches, IP leakage |
| **Vendor black boxes** | Can't audit third-party AI agent behavior | Liability when agents fail |
| **Regulatory uncertainty** | EU AI Act, state laws, industry regulations all diverging | Paralysis, missed opportunities |

### The Stakes Are Massive
- **Financial services:** $1.7B in AI-related fines in 2025 alone
- **Healthcare:** HIPAA violations now include AI agent behavior
- **EU AI Act:** 6% of global revenue penalties for non-compliance
- **SOX auditors:** Now requiring AI system documentation
- **Cyber insurance:** Policies excluding "autonomous AI actions"

### Why Current Solutions Fail
- **MLOps platforms:** Track model metrics, not agent behavior
- **GRC tools:** Designed for humans, not autonomous systems
- **AI security tools:** Focus on attacks, not compliance
- **Internal audit teams:** Don't have AI expertise, drowning in manual work
- **Consulting firms:** $500/hour, no continuous monitoring

**There is no SOC 2 for AI agents. No ISO standard. No trusted third-party certification that enterprises can show to regulators, customers, and boards.**
