## Executive Summary

Health tech is broken. We have 400,000+ health apps tracking isolated metrics—steps here, sleep there, heart rate somewhere else. But the human body isn't a collection of independent dashboards. It's an intricately connected system where cardiovascular, metabolic, neurological, immune, and musculoskeletal systems constantly interact.

**BodyOS** is the first AI operating system that models your entire physiology as a unified whole. By integrating data from wearables, labs, genetics, and medical records, BodyOS creates a living digital twin of your body's systems—understanding not just what's happening, but *why* it's happening and what's coming next.

This isn't another health app. It's the intelligence layer that makes sense of everything.

**$85B+ market opportunity. The future of personalized medicine. Category-defining.**
