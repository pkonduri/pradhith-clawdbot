The Crisis

Science Has a Trust Problem

AI-generated fake papers, data fabrication, and image manipulation are flooding journals at unprecedented scale

🤖

AI-Generated Papers

GPT-4 and successors generate convincing research with fabricated data, fake citations, and manipulated images—at industrial scale

🏭

Paper Mills

Organized operations selling fake research to academics under publish-or-perish pressure. Thousands of papers, hundreds of journals.

🖼️

Image Manipulation

Western blots, microscopy images, and graphs routinely duplicated or digitally altered. 35%+ of submissions show signs of manipulation.

🔗

Citation Cartels

Networks gaming impact factors through coordinated citation schemes. Entire journals compromised by manipulation rings.

📊

Data Fabrication

Statistical impossibilities and fabricated datasets going undetected for years. Manual review catches less than 30%.

⏰

Years Behind

Manual peer review takes 2-6 months. Retraction watchdogs are overwhelmed. Bad science pollutes the literature indefinitely.
