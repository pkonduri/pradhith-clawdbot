## The Problem

### The Great Wealth Transfer Is Here
- **$84 trillion** will transfer from Baby Boomers to heirs over the next two decades
- **67%** of Americans have no estate plan at all
- **Average estate attorney fees:** $3,000-$10,000+ for basic planning
- **Probate costs:** 3-7% of estate value plus 6-24 months of delays
- **$700+ billion** lost annually to unnecessary estate taxes, fees, and family disputes

### Estate Planning Is Broken
- **Prohibitively expensive:** Quality advice starts at $500/hour
- **Hopelessly complex:** 50 state jurisdictions, ever-changing tax laws
- **Dangerously static:** Plans become outdated as laws and circumstances change
- **Emotionally fraught:** 60% of families experience conflict during wealth transfer
- **Massively fragmented:** Wills, trusts, insurance, investments, property—all disconnected

### The Hidden Crisis
- **70% of wealth transfers fail** by the second generation
- **90% fail by the third generation**—not due to poor investments, but poor planning
- Families lose an average of **33%** of inherited wealth to taxes, fees, and disputes
- Most people learn about estate planning failures *after* it's too late

## The Solution

### Autonomous Estate Intelligence
InheritanceOS is the first AI system that continuously manages, optimizes, and executes wealth transfer strategies across the entire estate lifecycle.

**Core Capabilities:**

#### 1. Intelligent Estate Discovery
- Automatically aggregates all assets: financial accounts, property, digital assets, business interests
- Connects to banks, brokerages, property records, insurance carriers
- Builds a real-time, always-current picture of total estate value
- Identifies hidden assets and liabilities most people forget

#### 2. AI Legal Document Generation
- Generates state-compliant wills, trusts, powers of attorney, healthcare directives
- Updates automatically when laws change or circumstances shift
- Handles complex structures: revocable/irrevocable trusts, GRATs, QPRTs, CRTs
- Multi-jurisdictional support for families with property in multiple states

#### 3. Tax Optimization Engine
- Continuous analysis of federal and state estate tax exposure
- Identifies optimal gifting strategies (annual exclusion, lifetime exemption)
- Recommends trust structures that minimize tax burden
- Projects future tax liability under different scenarios

#### 4. Family Harmony Protocols
- AI-mediated family meetings and difficult conversations
- Equitable distribution modeling across all heirs
- Conflict prediction and prevention based on family dynamics
- Legacy letter and ethical will generation

#### 5. Autonomous Execution
- Integrates with legal e-signature and notarization
- Coordinates with attorneys, accountants, and financial advisors
- Manages beneficiary designations across all accounts
- Executes post-death administration with minimal human intervention
