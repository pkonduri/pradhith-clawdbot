# AgentInsure — The AI-Native Insurance Platform for Autonomous Agent Liability

## Executive Summary

As AI agents gain autonomy to make purchases, sign contracts, and take physical actions in the real world, the question of liability becomes mission-critical. Who pays when an AI agent makes a $50,000 error? When it crashes a delivery drone? When it sends the wrong email to a client and causes reputational damage?

**AgentInsure** is the first AI-native insurance platform designed specifically for autonomous agent liability. We don't just underwrite policies—we embed into the agent runtime, monitor behavior in real-time, price risk dynamically, and settle claims automatically. We're building the trust layer that enables the autonomous economy.

**The Opportunity:** The AI agent market is projected to reach $280B by 2028. With enterprise adoption accelerating, AI liability insurance is becoming mandatory—yet no platform exists to properly assess, price, and cover agent-specific risks. We're capturing this market at inception.

## The Problem

### The Liability Gap

Every day, AI agents are:
- Making financial transactions worth millions
- Sending communications on behalf of executives
- Controlling physical systems (drones, robots, vehicles)
- Accessing sensitive data and making decisions
- Negotiating and signing contracts

**When things go wrong—and they will—who's liable?**

Traditional insurance doesn't work:
- **E&O policies** don't cover autonomous AI decisions
- **Cyber insurance** focuses on breaches, not agent errors
- **General liability** excludes AI-specific scenarios
- **No real-time risk assessment** for dynamic agent behavior

### The Trust Barrier

Enterprises want to deploy AI agents, but face critical blockers:
- **Legal uncertainty:** Unclear liability frameworks
- **Risk quantification:** No way to measure agent reliability
- **Compliance gaps:** Regulators demanding coverage proof
- **Board hesitation:** "What if the AI does something catastrophic?"

### The Data Problem

Insurers want to enter this market, but can't:
- **No historical data** on AI agent failures
- **No standardized risk metrics** for agent behavior
- **No real-time visibility** into agent operations
- **No claims precedent** for AI-caused damages

### Core Products

#### 1. Agent E&O Coverage
Coverage for errors, omissions, and unintended consequences of AI agent decisions.

- **Financial errors:** Wrong transactions, pricing mistakes, calculation errors
- **Communication failures:** Incorrect emails, inappropriate responses, disclosure violations
- **Decision errors:** Bad recommendations, failed analysis, wrong conclusions
- **Coverage:** $100K - $50M per incident

#### 2. Physical Action Liability
Coverage for AI agents controlling physical systems.

- **Robotics:** Manufacturing errors, warehouse accidents, service robot incidents
- **Delivery:** Drone crashes, autonomous vehicle damage, delivery failures
- **Infrastructure:** HVAC systems, security systems, building automation
- **Coverage:** $500K - $100M per incident

#### 3. Transaction Protection
Coverage for financial transactions executed by AI agents.

- **Payment errors:** Wrong amounts, wrong recipients, duplicate transactions
- **Contract issues:** Unauthorized agreements, unfavorable terms
- **Trading losses:** Algorithmic trading failures, execution errors
- **Coverage:** Up to $250M per incident

#### 4. Multi-Agent Coordination
Coverage for complex multi-agent systems and swarms.

- **Coordination failures:** Agents working at cross-purposes
- **Cascade errors:** One agent's failure triggering system-wide issues
- **Emergent behavior:** Unexpected outcomes from agent interactions
- **Coverage:** Custom structured policies
