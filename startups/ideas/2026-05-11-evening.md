## The Problem

### The Cold Chain Crisis

**Temperature-sensitive logistics is broken:**

1. **Massive waste:** $35B+ in pharmaceutical cold chain failures annually. 25% of vaccines arrive degraded. 40% of food is lost to cold chain breaks.

2. **Fragmented visibility:** Shipments cross 5-15 handoff points. Each uses different monitoring systems. Data silos everywhere.

3. **Reactive, not predictive:** Current systems alert AFTER temperature excursions. By then, the product is already compromised.

4. **Compliance nightmare:** FDA, EMA, WHO, HACCP, GDP, GMP — each has temperature documentation requirements. Manual compliance costs $500K-$2M/year for mid-size pharma.

5. **Rising complexity:** mRNA vaccines need -70°C. Cell therapies need -150°C cryogenic transport. Personalized medicine means smaller, more complex shipments.

6. **Climate volatility:** Extreme heat events are increasing. Traditional cold chain infrastructure wasn't designed for 115°F ambient temperatures.

### Real Consequences

- **Moderna lost $400M** in vaccine doses due to cold chain failures in 2021
- **CAR-T cell therapies** ($500K per dose) have 5-8% transport failure rates
- **Organ transplants** fail at 20%+ rates partly due to cold chain issues
- **Food waste** from temperature excursions costs $165B annually in the US alone
