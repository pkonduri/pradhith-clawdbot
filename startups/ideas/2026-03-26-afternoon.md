*Trust, but verify. Now you can.*

## Executive Summary

**ReasonTrace AI** is the enterprise platform that captures, verifies, and certifies AI reasoning chains for regulatory compliance, legal defensibility, and trust. As AI agents make millions of autonomous decisions in high-stakes domains—approving loans, diagnosing patients, executing trades, drafting contracts—organizations need audit-grade proof of WHY each decision was made. ReasonTrace provides the forensic infrastructure to reconstruct, verify, and defend any AI decision.

**The Problem:** AI systems make decisions, but can't prove why. When regulators ask "Why was this loan denied?", when lawsuits demand "What reasoning led to this diagnosis?", when auditors require "Show the decision trail"—enterprises have nothing but black boxes and probability scores.

**The Solution:** A reasoning capture and verification layer that integrates with any AI system to create tamper-proof, audit-grade trails of every decision—including the data considered, reasoning steps taken, alternatives evaluated, and confidence levels at each stage.

**Market Opportunity:** $65B AI audit and compliance market by 2030. Every regulated industry (finance, healthcare, legal, insurance, government) will require AI decision audit trails by 2028.

**Business Model:** Enterprise SaaS ($200K-$2M ARR per enterprise) + Per-decision pricing for high-volume deployments + Compliance certification revenue.

### Core Capabilities

#### 1. Universal Reasoning Capture

ReasonTrace integrates with any AI system:

**For LLMs/Foundation Models:**
- Automatic chain-of-thought extraction
- Structured reasoning step capture
- Prompt and context preservation
- Token-level attribution tracking

**For Traditional ML:**
- Decision tree path reconstruction
- Feature importance at decision time
- Model state snapshots
- Training data provenance

**For Multi-Agent Systems:**
- Agent communication logging
- Delegation and escalation chains
- Consensus mechanism documentation
- Inter-agent reasoning graphs

#### 2. Reasoning Verification

Every captured reasoning chain is verified:

**Logical Consistency Check:**
- Does step B logically follow from step A?
- Are there contradictions in the reasoning?
- Is the conclusion supported by the steps?

**Factual Grounding:**
- What data points were considered?
- Were the facts accurate at decision time?
- What sources were consulted?

**Bias Audit:**
- Protected class analysis
- Disparate impact detection
- Proxy discrimination identification

**Counterfactual Analysis:**
- "What would have changed the decision?"
- Sensitivity analysis on key factors
- Alternative outcome modeling

#### 3. Immutable Audit Infrastructure

**Cryptographic Guarantees:**
- Every reasoning trace is cryptographically signed
- Tamper-evident logging with blockchain anchoring
- Time-stamped with trusted third-party attestation
- Chain of custody for regulatory submissions

**Retention & Retrieval:**
- Configurable retention (7 years+ for regulated industries)
- Sub-second retrieval for any decision
- Bulk export for regulatory examinations
- Litigation hold automation

#### 4. Explanation Generation

**Multiple Audiences, One Truth:**

| Audience | Explanation Type | Example |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| **Consumer** | Plain language | "Your loan was denied because your debt-to-income ratio of 48% exceeds our 43% threshold" |
| **Regulator** | Compliance format | Full ECOA adverse action notice with all considered factors |
| **Legal** | Defense documentation | Complete reasoning chain with supporting evidence |
| **Technical** | Deep audit | Token-level attribution, feature weights, model state |
