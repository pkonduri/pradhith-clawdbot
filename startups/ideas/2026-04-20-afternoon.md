# FoodNet AI — The Intelligence Layer for Global Food Security

*Securing the world's food supply with end-to-end predictive intelligence*

---

## The Billion-Dollar Opportunity

**The global food system is the largest, most fragile supply chain on Earth.**

The food industry generates $8.5 trillion annually. Yet it operates on:
- **Guesswork**: Farmers plant based on last year's prices, not next year's demand
- **Opacity**: 40% of food is wasted because nobody knows where it is or when it expires
- **Fragility**: A drought in Brazil, a war in Ukraine, or a port closure can trigger global famine
- **Disconnection**: Commodity traders, food processors, retailers, and governments all operate in silos

**The result?**
- 830 million people face hunger
- $1.2 trillion in annual food waste
- Price spikes that destabilize nations
- Supply shocks every 2-3 years that cost billions

**FoodNet AI** is the first end-to-end intelligence platform that connects every node in the global food system — from seed to shelf — delivering predictive visibility, real-time optimization, and crisis-proof resilience.

## Product Architecture

### 1. YieldMind — Predictive Crop Intelligence
- **Satellite + IoT fusion**: Planet Labs imagery + weather data + soil sensors → yield predictions 6 months out
- **500+ crop models**: From wheat to quinoa to almonds, trained on 50 years of USDA/FAO data
- **Regional granularity**: County-level predictions in 180+ countries
- **Climate adaptation**: Factor in drought, flood, heat stress, and pest migration patterns
- **Confidence intervals**: Probability distributions, not single-point forecasts

### 2. FlowGraph — Global Food Supply Chain Visibility
- **End-to-end tracking**: From farm to processor to distributor to retailer to consumer
- **Multi-modal logistics**: Shipping containers, rail, trucking, cold chain — all in one view
- **Real-time positions**: 10M+ shipments tracked via AIS, GPS, and EDI integration
- **Spoilage prediction**: ML models estimate remaining shelf life based on temperature history
- **Bottleneck detection**: Identify congestion at ports, borders, and distribution centers before it's critical

### 3. PriceGrid — Real-Time Food Commodity Intelligence
- **Live pricing**: Ingest from 200+ exchanges, auctions, and wholesale markets globally
- **Forward curves**: Generate implied futures from weather, inventory, and demand signals
- **Basis analytics**: Spot vs. futures, regional vs. global price differentials
- **Arbitrage detection**: Identify mispricing between regions and markets
- **Volatility forecasting**: Predict price swings before they happen

### 4. ShelfSense — Retail & Foodservice Optimization
- **Demand sensing**: Use POS data, weather, events, and social signals to predict daily demand
- **Dynamic ordering**: Auto-generate purchase orders optimized for freshness and margin
- **Waste prevention**: Alert on slow-moving inventory before it expires
- **Assortment intelligence**: Recommend SKU mix based on local preferences and availability
- **Promotion optimization**: Predict lift from price changes and marketing

### 5. CrisisNet — Early Warning & Resilience Platform
- **Famine prediction**: 90-day advance warning of food security crises by region
- **Scenario modeling**: Simulate impact of droughts, conflicts, trade policy changes
- **Alternative sourcing**: Auto-identify substitute suppliers when primary sources fail
- **Government dashboard**: Food security intelligence for ministries and UN agencies
- **Emergency routing**: Optimize humanitarian food distribution in crisis zones

### 6. ComplianceHub — Regulatory & Sustainability Intelligence
- **Traceability**: Farm-to-fork provenance for food safety and recalls
- **Certification tracking**: Organic, Fair Trade, Rainforest Alliance verification
- **Emissions accounting**: Scope 3 carbon footprint per product
- **Deforestation monitoring**: Supply chain exposure to illegal land clearing
- **Regulatory alerts**: FSMA, EU Farm-to-Fork, and 50+ country requirements

### 1. Climate Volatility is Exploding
- 2024-2025: Record droughts in Amazon, floods in Pakistan, heat waves in Europe
- Crop failures increasing 3x faster than historical baseline
- $80B+ in annual agricultural losses from climate events

### 2. Geopolitical Food Weaponization
- Russia-Ukraine conflict showed food can be a weapon
- Export bans and strategic reserves reshaping trade flows
- Nations scrambling to secure supply chains

### 3. Technology Convergence
- Daily satellite imagery now available at $0.50/km²
- IoT sensors cheap enough to deploy at farm scale
- LLMs can process shipping documents, contracts, weather reports automatically

### 4. ESG & Regulatory Pressure
- EU Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive (CSDDD) requires supply chain visibility
- Deforestation regulations forcing traceability
- Scope 3 emissions reporting mandatory for food companies

### 5. Private Markets Pouring into Food
- Farmland became a $10T+ asset class
- Food & Ag VC hit $30B+ annually
- Institutional investors need data to deploy capital
