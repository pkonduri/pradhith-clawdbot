## The Billion-Dollar Problem

Every human operates on a 24-hour biological clock that controls nearly every function in the body — metabolism, hormone release, cognitive performance, immune function, and cellular repair. The 2017 Nobel Prize in Medicine validated what science now confirms: **chronobiology is fundamental to human health**.

Yet we live in a world designed to destroy our circadian rhythms:
- **56% of adults** report their sleep schedule conflicts with their work schedule
- **$411 billion** lost annually in the US alone due to insufficient sleep
- **70% of shift workers** suffer from circadian rhythm disorders
- **Chronotype mismatch** (when work schedules conflict with biology) costs employers $2,000+ per employee per year
- **Medication effectiveness** varies up to 400% depending on time of administration

Current solutions fail because they focus on **single symptoms** (sleep apps, light boxes, meal timing apps) rather than the **integrated biological system**. There is no unified operating system for human chronobiological optimization.
