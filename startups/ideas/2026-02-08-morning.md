**Step 2: Army Mobilization**
- Commander analyzes mission, breaks into epics

- Squads receive assignments, spin up in sandboxed environments

**Step 3: Autonomous Development**
- Agents work in parallel across the codebase
- Continuous integration catches conflicts
- QA validates every commit
- Security scans run continuously
- Progress streams to War Room dashboard

**Step 4: Human Checkpoints**
- Architecture review before major components
- Security sign-off before production
- Business validation at milestones
- Full audit trail for compliance

**Step 5: Continuous Deployment**
- Auto-deploy to staging on passing tests
- Canary releases to production
- Agents monitor and respond to incidents
- Continuous maintenance and optimization
