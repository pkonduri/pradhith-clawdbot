### The Rating Framework

**BenchmarkScore™** — The universal AI rating (0-1000):

| Score | Grade | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 900+ | AAA | Exceptional across all dimensions |
| 800-899 | AA | Very high quality, minimal concerns |
| 700-799 | A | High quality, suitable for most uses |
| 600-699 | BBB | Adequate quality, some limitations |
| 500-599 | BB | Acceptable with caveats |
| 400-499 | B | Below average, significant concerns |
| <400 | C/D | High risk, not recommended |

**Sub-ratings** for specialized assessment:
- **Capability Index (CI)**: Raw performance on standardized tasks
- **Safety Index (SI)**: Resistance to misuse and harmful outputs
- **Reliability Index (RI)**: Consistency and accuracy over time
- **Transparency Index (TI)**: Explainability and auditability
- **Compliance Index (COI)**: Regulatory conformity
