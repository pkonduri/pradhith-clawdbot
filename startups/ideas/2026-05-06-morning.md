## The Billion-Dollar Problem

**Enterprises are drowning in AI chaos.**

The average Fortune 500 company now has **400+ AI models** in production — and growing 3x year-over-year. But there's no system of record. No single source of truth. No way to answer basic questions like:

- Which models are deployed where?
- What data trained each model?
- Who approved this model for production?
- Are we compliant with the EU AI Act?
- How much are we spending on inference?
- What happens if a training dataset is found to be tainted?

**This is the messy reality:**

- Models scattered across AWS, Azure, GCP, and on-prem
- Training data in S3, Snowflake, Databricks — and nobody knows what was used
- Shadow AI proliferating in every department
- Compliance teams manually chasing down model cards
- Security has no visibility into model vulnerabilities
- Finance can't attribute AI costs to business units

**The consequences are catastrophic:**

- **$2.1B**: Average cost of AI compliance violations (post-EU AI Act)
- **67%**: Enterprises that can't audit their AI model inventory
- **45 days**: Average time to respond to AI regulatory inquiries
- **$340M**: Largest AI-related fine to date (discrimination in lending)

Without a system of record for AI, enterprises are flying blind into a regulatory buzzsaw.

## The Solution: ModelLedger

**The enterprise system of record for AI assets.**

ModelLedger provides:

### 🗂️ **Universal Model Registry**
- **Auto-discovery**: Automatically find and catalog every AI model across all environments
- **Version control**: Track every model version with full lineage
- **Metadata management**: Rich tagging, search, and organization
- **Cross-platform**: One registry across AWS, Azure, GCP, Databricks, Snowflake, and on-prem

### 🔗 **Complete Data Lineage**
- **Training data tracking**: Know exactly what data trained each model
- **Data provenance**: Full chain of custody for every dataset
- **Impact analysis**: When data is flagged, instantly identify affected models
- **PII tracking**: Automated detection and documentation of sensitive data usage

### ✅ **Compliance Automation**
- **EU AI Act**: Automated risk classification, documentation, and reporting
- **NIST AI RMF**: Built-in framework mapping and gap analysis
- **Model cards**: Auto-generated, always up-to-date documentation
- **Audit trails**: Every action logged for regulatory inquiry response

### 📊 **Operational Intelligence**
- **Performance monitoring**: Track model drift, accuracy, and health
- **Cost attribution**: Per-model, per-inference cost tracking by business unit
- **Usage analytics**: Who's using what, when, and how
- **Anomaly detection**: Early warning for model degradation or misuse

### 🛡️ **Risk Management**
- **Bias detection**: Continuous fairness monitoring
- **Security scanning**: Vulnerability assessment for models and dependencies
- **Access control**: Fine-grained permissions and audit logging
- **Incident response**: Rapid model quarantine and rollback capabilities
