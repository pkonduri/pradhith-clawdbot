## How It Works

### The DeepSim Process

**Step 1: Define the Decision**
```
Decision: Market Entry — Launch in Germany
Timeline: Q3 2026
Investment: €50M
Key Questions:
- Direct vs. partnership model?
- Premium or mid-market positioning?
- Berlin HQ or distributed?
```

**Step 2: Build the Simulation World**
DeepSim automatically constructs:
- Your company model (from financials, org structure, capabilities)
- German market dynamics (TAM, growth, regulation)
- Competitor landscape (existing players, likely responses)
- Customer segments (needs, price sensitivity, adoption curves)
- Macro factors (currency, political, economic)

**Step 3: Run Simulations**
- 10,000+ scenario permutations
- Each scenario runs 5-year projections
- Monte Carlo uncertainty modeling
- Competitive game theory responses
- Sensitivity analysis on all variables

**Step 4: Synthesize Insights**
```
SIMULATION RESULTS: Germany Market Entry

Expected Outcomes (50th percentile):
├── Year 1 Revenue: €12M (Range: €6-22M)
├── Break-even: Month 34 (Range: 24-48)
├── Market Share Year 5: 8.2% (Range: 4-15%)
└── 5-Year NPV: €45M (Range: -€20M to €180M)

Key Risks Identified:
├── 82% probability: Competitor X responds with price cut
├── 45% probability: Regulatory delay of 6+ months
├── 34% probability: Key talent acquisition challenges
└── 23% probability: Currency headwinds exceed 10%

Strategic Recommendations:
├── Partnership model reduces risk by 40%
├── Premium positioning outperforms in 67% of scenarios
├── Delaying to Q1 2027 improves outcomes by 15%
└── €65M investment threshold for 80% success probability
```

**Step 5: Continuous Monitoring**
- Assumptions tracked against reality
- Automatic re-simulation on material changes
- Decision performance tracking
