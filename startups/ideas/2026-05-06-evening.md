**The dental industry is the last major healthcare vertical without an AI operating system.**

## The Vision: DentalOS

DentalOS is the **unified AI intelligence platform** that transforms dental practices from reactive treatment centers into **predictive oral health engines**.

### Core Platform Components

#### 1. **DiagnosticAI Engine**
- Real-time analysis of X-rays, CBCT scans, intraoral photos
- Detection: cavities, periodontal disease, bone loss, oral cancer, TMJ disorders
- Confidence scoring and attention mapping
- Longitudinal tracking across patient history

#### 2. **TreatmentMind**
- AI-generated treatment plans optimized for outcomes + patient preferences
- Multi-scenario modeling (conservative vs. aggressive approaches)
- Cost estimation with insurance coverage prediction
- Phased treatment sequencing

#### 3. **InsuranceAI**
- Automatic pre-authorization with optimal documentation
- Claim denial prediction and prevention
- Appeal automation with 85%+ success rate
- Fee schedule optimization across payers

#### 4. **PatientBridge**
- Conversational AI for scheduling, reminders, and education
- Treatment acceptance optimization through personalized communication
- Fear/anxiety detection and intervention protocols
- Financial planning and payment optimization

#### 5. **PracticeOS**
- Unified dashboard across all practice systems
- Staff scheduling and productivity optimization
- Supply chain prediction and auto-ordering
- Revenue cycle management and analytics

#### 6. **OralGenome**
- Integration with genetic risk factors for caries, periodontal disease
- Microbiome analysis for personalized prevention
- Saliva biomarker monitoring
- Whole-body health correlation (oral-systemic link)
