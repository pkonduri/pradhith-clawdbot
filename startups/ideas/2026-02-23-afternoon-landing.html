How It Works

Three Steps to Privacy-First Verification

1

User Enrolls Once

User verifies identity with a trusted issuer (DMV, bank, employer). Issuer creates cryptographic attestation stored only in user's wallet. Original documents never leave their device.

2

Proof Generated

When a website requests verification ("Is user 18+?"), ProofLayer SDK generates a zero-knowledge proof on-device. The proof mathematically proves the claim without revealing any underlying data.

3

Instant Verification

Site verifies the cryptographic proof in under 50ms. Full compliance audit trail, zero PII transmitted or stored. Everyone wins.
