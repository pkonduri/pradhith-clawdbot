## The Billion-Dollar Insight

**70% of successful startups begin with a solo founder** — yet the entire startup ecosystem (VCs, accelerators, advisors) is designed around the assumption you'll have a co-founding team.

Solo founders face an impossible choice:
- **Go technical?** You'll struggle with sales, fundraising, hiring, and business development
- **Go business?** You'll hemorrhage money on contractors, get technical debt, ship slower
- **Find a co-founder?** Median time is 8-14 months. 65% of startups fail due to co-founder conflict anyway

**CoFounder AI** is the first AI operating system purpose-built for solo founders — giving you the capabilities of a complete founding team without the equity dilution, conflicts, or vesting drama.
