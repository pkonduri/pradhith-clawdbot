## 💡 The Problem

### The $500 Trillion Opportunity with Massive Friction

Real-world assets (RWA) represent over **$500 trillion in global value**—real estate, bonds, commodities, art, infrastructure, private equity. Yet less than 0.1% has been tokenized.

**Why hasn't tokenization exploded yet?**

1. **Regulatory Labyrinth**: Different jurisdictions have conflicting rules for digital securities. A single tokenized asset might need to comply with 50+ regulatory frameworks.

2. **Valuation Chaos**: No standardized, real-time valuation methodologies for diverse asset classes. Manual appraisals are expensive, slow, and inconsistent.

3. **Legal Complexity**: Token structures require custom legal work for each asset type and jurisdiction—costing $100K-$500K per tokenization.

4. **Fragmented Infrastructure**: Custody, issuance, trading, and settlement systems don't talk to each other. Assets get stuck in silos.

5. **Liquidity Black Holes**: Secondary markets for tokenized assets are illiquid, fragmented, and unreliable.

6. **Compliance Overhead**: Ongoing reporting, KYC/AML, investor eligibility, and tax compliance require armies of professionals.

### The Result?
- Average tokenization cost: $250K-$1M per asset
- Time to market: 6-18 months
- 90% of tokenization projects fail before launch
- Only large institutions can afford to participate

**The $10 trillion market is waiting for someone to make tokenization as easy as launching a website.**
