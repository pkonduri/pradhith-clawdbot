### Fashion's Trillion-Dollar Circular Crisis

The fashion industry is collapsing under its own weight:

**1. The Authentication Catastrophe**
- **$30B** in counterfeit fashion sold annually—growing 15% YoY
- Luxury brands lose **$23B** to fakes yearly
- Current authentication is manual, expensive ($20-50 per item), and bottlenecked
- NFT/blockchain authentication failed—too complex, poor UX, no consumer adoption

**2. The Compliance Avalanche**
- **EU Digital Product Passport (DPP)** mandatory by 2027—every fashion item needs a digital twin
- 35+ countries implementing similar regulations by 2028
- Brands have **18 months** to achieve full supply chain traceability

- Resale market hitting **$77B in 2026**, projected **$200B by 2030**
- But 60% of resale transactions involve authentication friction
- Pricing is wildly inconsistent—same item varies 300% across platforms

- 85% of consumers want to buy sustainably, only 20% can verify claims
- Greenwashing lawsuits up 400% since 2023
- Carbon footprint tracking requires supply chain data most brands don't have
- No trusted standard for circular fashion claims
