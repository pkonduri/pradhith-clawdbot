## The Problem: A $35B Market Running on 1990s Technology

### The Broken Status Quo

**Empty Leg Economics**
- 40% of private jet flights are empty repositioning legs
- $3.5 billion wasted annually on flying empty planes
- Matching supply with demand is manual and inefficient
- Brokers list empty legs but matching is primitive

**Charter Booking Chaos**
- Average booking takes 47 emails/calls across 12 operators
- Pricing is opaque—same route varies 300% between quotes
- No real-time availability visibility
- Brokers optimize for commission, not client value

**Corporate Flight Department Inefficiency**
- Fortune 500 companies spend $8-15M annually on flight departments
- Aircraft utilization averages just 400 hours/year (vs. 1,200+ optimal)
- Manual crew scheduling, maintenance coordination, trip planning
- CFOs have zero visibility into true cost-per-flight

**Maintenance & Safety Fragmentation**
- Unscheduled maintenance causes 23% of flight cancellations
- Parts sourcing averages 4.7 days for AOG (aircraft on ground)
- Safety management systems are paper-based at 60% of operators
- Regulatory compliance is a full-time manual job
