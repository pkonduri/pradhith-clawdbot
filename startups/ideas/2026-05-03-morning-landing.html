The Problem

A Goldmine Trapped in a Black Box

300+ GWh of EV batteries will retire by 2030. They're not dead—they retain 70-80% capacity. But there's no way to unlock their value.

No Standardization

Every battery is unique. Different chemistries, manufacturers, usage histories. No universal way to assess quality.

Information Asymmetry

Sellers know battery history. Buyers are flying blind. Trust doesn't exist in this market.

No Price Discovery

What's a used battery worth? Nobody knows. Fragmented brokers, opaque negotiations, massive value destruction.

Financing Gap

Banks won't lend against assets they can't value. Insurance won't cover risks they can't quantify.
