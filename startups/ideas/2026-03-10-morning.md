The global lobbying industry generates **$4B+ annually in the US alone**, with corporations, trade associations, and advocacy groups spending billions more on government affairs. Yet this industry operates on:

- Manual monitoring of thousands of bills across jurisdictions
- Relationship rolodexes managed in spreadsheets
- Intuition-based timing for engagement
- Expensive armies of former staffers and consultants

**LobbyOS transforms government affairs from art to science** — providing the intelligence infrastructure for organizations to navigate the complex web of policy, politics, and influence at machine scale.

- **Committee Power**: Who controls what, markup calendars, hearing schedules
