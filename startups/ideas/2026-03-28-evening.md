## The Product

### Core Intelligence Engine

**1. Cognitive State Detection**
- Ambient sensing of focus/distraction patterns
- Keyboard dynamics, mouse movements, app switching frequency
- Optional biometric integration (HRV, EDA, eye tracking)
- Real-time "brain weather" dashboard

**2. Adaptive Task Management**
- Voice-first task capture (no friction for working memory limitations)
- AI automatically breaks tasks into dopamine-sized chunks
- Dynamic prioritization based on energy state
- "Task morphing" — reframes tasks when resistance detected

**3. Hyperfocus Orchestration**
- Detects onset of hyperfocus states
- Protects focus windows (intelligent notification batching)
- Ensures hyperfocus targets important tasks (not rabbit holes)
- Gentle wind-down before burnout

**4. Transition Engineering**
- The hardest part of ADHD: starting and stopping tasks
- AI-designed "launch sequences" personalized to each user
- Friction reduction for task initiation
- Context-switching protocols that preserve state

**5. Time Blindness Correction**
- Proactive time estimation with personal calibration
- "Time remaining" ambient awareness
- Transition warnings with appropriate lead time
- Meeting/deadline approach alerts tuned to your needs

### Interface Philosophy

**Radical Simplicity**
- No complex dashboards (overwhelming = abandoned)
- Ambient intelligence that whispers, not shouts
- Voice-first, screen-second
- Invisible when not needed

**Dopamine-Aware Design**
- Variable reward schedules (gamification that works with ADHD brains)
- Progress visualization calibrated to motivate without overwhelming
- Celebration of "good enough" (perfectionism intervention)
- Failure reframing and compassionate nudges

**Context Modes**
- Work mode: Productivity optimization
- Creative mode: Idea capture without structure pressure
- Recovery mode: Post-hyperfocus gentle scheduling
- Crisis mode: Emergency task triage for overwhelmed states
