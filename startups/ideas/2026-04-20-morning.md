## Product Architecture

### 1. ForestScan Engine — Autonomous Forest Monitoring
- **Multi-source sensing**: Satellite (Sentinel-2, Planet Labs), LiDAR, drone-based hyperspectral
- **Species identification**: Deep learning models identify 500+ tree species from imagery
- **Biomass estimation**: Volumetric AI calculates board-feet and carbon storage per hectare
- **Change detection**: Weekly diff analysis catches illegal logging, fire damage, pest spread
- **Growth prediction**: ML models project 10-year growth curves by stand

### 2. PriceGrid — Real-Time Timber Market Intelligence
- **Live pricing**: Ingest mill bids, auction results, transport costs → dynamic pricing per species/region
- **Forward curves**: Generate timber futures implied by weather, demand, inventory
- **Arbitrage alerts**: Spot pricing gaps between regions and mills
- **Supply/demand modeling**: Predict shortages 6-12 months out
- **Integration**: API feeds to commodity traders, REITs, forestry funds

### 3. CarbonProof — Autonomous Carbon Credit Verification
- **Automated MRV**: Measurement, Reporting, Verification without $100K consultants
- **Additionality proofs**: AI-generated counterfactuals demonstrate real sequestration
- **Permanence monitoring**: Continuous monitoring ensures credits stay valid
- **Registry integration**: Direct issuance to Verra, Gold Standard, Puro.earth
- **Credit marketplace**: Match forest owners with corporate buyers

### 4. RiskShield — Predictive Forest Risk Intelligence
- **Fire probability**: 7-day fire risk scores using weather, fuel load, ignition history
- **Pest detection**: Early warning for bark beetle, invasive species spread
- **Climate projection**: Model forest health under various climate scenarios
- **Insurance pricing**: Parametric triggers for forest insurance products
- **Mitigation recommendations**: Optimal thinning schedules, firebreak placement

### 5. ChainTrace — Timber Supply Chain Verification
- **Tree-to-product tracking**: DNA/isotope fingerprinting + satellite verification
- **Deforestation-free proofs**: Demonstrate EUDR compliance automatically
- **Chain-of-custody**: From harvest to mill to retailer with immutable audit trail
- **Sustainability scoring**: ESG-ready certifications for institutional investors
