### The $10 Trillion Infrastructure Gap

Fusion power plants are the most complex machines ever built by humanity. A single SPARC-class reactor contains:
- Plasma at 100+ million degrees Celsius
- Superconducting magnets at -270Â°C
- Thousands of sensors generating petabytes of data daily
- Real-time control systems operating at microsecond precision
- Materials experiencing neutron bombardment unprecedented outside stars

**Current Reality:**
- **No unified software platform**: Each fusion company builds proprietary systems from scratch
