## The Problem: The AI Model Supply Chain Crisis

### The Hidden Attack Surface

Every enterprise now runs hundreds to thousands of AI models:
- Foundation models from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, Mistral, and dozens of others
- Open-source models from Hugging Face (1M+ models, largely unvetted)
- Fine-tuned variants from internal teams and third-party vendors
- Agent systems calling multiple models with complex dependencies

**Nobody knows what's actually in these models.**

### The Threat Landscape

| Attack Vector | Impact | 2025-2026 Growth |
|---------------|--------|------------------|
| **Model Poisoning** | Compromised outputs, data exfiltration | +340% YoY |
| **Backdoor Injection** | Targeted manipulation, hidden behaviors | +520% YoY |
| **Weight Tampering** | Subtle bias injection, degraded performance | +280% YoY |
| **Dependency Hijacking** | Malicious model substitution | +420% YoY |
| **Provenance Fraud** | Fake model origins, license violations | +380% YoY |

### The Regulatory Tsunami

- **EU AI Act (2026):** Mandatory AI system traceability and supply chain documentation
- **NIST AI RMF 2.0:** Model provenance requirements for federal contractors
- **SEC AI Disclosure Rules:** Public companies must disclose AI model risks
- **California AI Transparency Act:** Model origin tracking for consumer-facing AI
- **China's AI Governance Framework:** Strict model registration and audit requirements

### Current Solutions Are Inadequate

| Approach | Limitation |
|----------|-----------|
| **Manual audits** | Don't scale, point-in-time only |
| **Model cards** | Self-reported, easily falsified |
| **Internal testing** | Misses sophisticated attacks |
| **Vendor questionnaires** | No verification, compliance theater |
| **Existing MLOps** | Focused on deployment, not security |

**The market is desperate for a real solution.**

### The Trust Infrastructure for AI Model Supply Chains

FoundationGuard provides enterprise-grade security and provenance verification for every AI model in your organization.

### Core Capabilities

#### 1. **Model DNA Fingerprinting™**
Cryptographic proof of model identity that persists across:
- Model exports and imports
- Fine-tuning and distillation
- Quantization and optimization
- Format conversions (ONNX, TensorRT, etc.)

*No more "I think this is the same model we approved"—mathematical certainty.*

#### 2. **Provenance Chain Verification**
Complete lineage tracking from base model to deployment:
- Training data attestations
- Fine-tuning audit trails
- Transfer learning history
- All modifications cryptographically signed

*Know exactly where every model came from and what happened to it.*

#### 3. **Real-Time Integrity Monitoring**
Continuous verification that deployed models haven't been:
- Tampered with at rest
- Modified during loading
- Substituted at inference time
- Degraded through weight manipulation

*Catch attacks in milliseconds, not months.*

#### 4. **Automated Compliance Engine**
One-click compliance with:
- EU AI Act supply chain requirements
- NIST AI RMF model management
- SOC 2 AI addendum controls
- Industry-specific regulations (HIPAA AI, FDA SaMD)

*Generate audit-ready documentation automatically.*

#### 5. **Model SBOM (Software Bill of Materials)**
Complete dependency mapping:
- Base model identification
- Training dataset provenance
- Library and framework versions
- Embedded component analysis

*The SBOM movement, purpose-built for AI.*

#### 6. **Threat Intelligence Network**
Collective defense across the ecosystem:
- Known-bad model signatures
- Emerging attack pattern detection
- Community-reported vulnerabilities
- Proactive security alerts

*Benefit from the security of the entire network.*
