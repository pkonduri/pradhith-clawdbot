// The Challenge

Space Habitats Are Impossibly Complex

Keeping humans alive in the most hostile environment demands perfection — and current systems can't deliver

⚠️

Life-or-Death Systems

A single ISS module contains 1,400+ sensors and 350+ equipment items. One cascade failure can kill the crew in minutes. Current systems react after problems occur.

📦

Resource Scarcity

Every kilogram to orbit costs $2,700-54,000. Water, oxygen, food — everything is precious. Current systems waste resources through suboptimal management.

🧠

Crew Overload

Astronauts spend 30%+ of their time on maintenance and monitoring. They make 1,000+ decisions daily. That's time stolen from science and exploration.

👤

Human Factors

Isolation, radiation, microgravity — constant stress. Medical emergencies with no hospital for weeks. Mental health directly impacts mission success.
