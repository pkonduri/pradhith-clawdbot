## The Problem

### 1. The Plastic Pollution Crisis
- **400M tonnes** produced annually; **350M tonnes** become waste
- Only **9%** is recycled globally; 22% is mismanaged (landfills, oceans)
- **8 million tonnes** enter oceans annually
- Microplastics now found in human blood, breast milk, and brains

### 2. Regulatory Tsunami
- **EU Plastic Tax:** €0.80/kg on non-recycled plastic packaging (2021+)
- **EPR Laws:** 30+ countries mandate producers fund end-of-life costs
- **US State Laws:** California, Maine, Oregon require recycled content mandates
- **Corporate Commitments:** 500+ companies pledged 25% recycled content by 2025

### 3. The Verification Void
- No standardized plastic credit methodology (fragmented standards)
- Rampant greenwashing — brands buy dubious "plastic neutrality"
- Recyclers can't prove chain of custody
- EPR compliance is manual, expensive, error-prone

### 4. Market Inefficiency
- No transparent pricing for plastic credits
- Recyclers underpaid; brands overpay intermediaries
- Quality mismatches (food-grade vs. downcycled)
- Geographic arbitrage invisible
