## The RehabOS Solution

**RehabOS is the autonomous intelligence layer that makes every patient's recovery continuous, personalized, and measurable.**

### Core Platform Components

#### 1. RehabVision — Computer Vision Movement Analysis
- Uses any smartphone camera (no special hardware)
- Tracks joint angles, movement quality, compensation patterns
- Real-time form correction with voice/visual feedback
- Identifies subtle deterioration before patient notices
- Works offline, processes on-device for privacy

#### 2. RehabMind — Autonomous Exercise Intelligence
- Generates personalized exercise progressions based on actual performance
- Adapts difficulty in real-time based on movement quality
- Predicts optimal exercise timing based on recovery patterns
- Creates progressive overload plans that match healing trajectory

#### 3. RehabLink — Care Coordination Layer
- Unified dashboard for entire care team
- Automatic progress reports to referring physicians
- Insurance documentation automation
- Family/caregiver visibility into recovery progress
- Seamless handoffs between care settings

#### 4. RehabCoach — AI Motivation & Accountability
- Conversational AI coach available 24/7
- Understands pain vs. productive discomfort
- Behavioral psychology-based motivation
- Detects signs of depression/anxiety affecting recovery
- Connects to human therapist when needed

#### 5. RehabPredict — Outcome Intelligence
- ML models predict recovery trajectory
- Early warning for complications (re-tear, chronic pain risk)
- Insurance pre-authorization automation
- Evidence-based treatment plan optimization
- Population health insights for health systems
