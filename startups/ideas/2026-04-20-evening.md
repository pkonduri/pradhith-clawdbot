## Core Product Capabilities

### 1. Intelligent Site Assessment
Before any restoration begins, RegenOS analyzes:
- **Historical ecosystem state** (what was here before degradation)
- **Current soil health** via spectral analysis and eDNA
- **Hydrology patterns** from satellite and sensor data
- **Adjacent ecosystem connectivity** for corridor planning
- **Climate trajectory** for species selection resilience
- **Social factors** including land rights and community needs

**Output:** A probabilistic restoration roadmap with multiple scenario pathways.

### 2. Species Selection Engine
Our AI selects optimal species mixes based on:
- Native ecosystem templates
- Climate-resilient genetics
- Biodiversity value scores
- Carbon sequestration potential
- Economic value for local communities
- Pollinator and wildlife corridor integration

We don't do monocultures. Every RegenOS project is designed for biodiverse, self-sustaining ecosystems.

### 3. Restoration Execution Intelligence
AI agents optimize:
- **Planting timing** based on hyper-local weather prediction
- **Intervention sequences** (soil prep → pioneer species → climax species)
- **Resource allocation** across multiple sites
- **Adaptive management** based on real-time monitoring
- **Early warning** for disease, drought, fire, invasive species

### 4. Continuous Monitoring Fusion
We integrate every sensing modality:
- **Satellite imagery** (daily to weekly, multispectral + radar)
- **Drone surveys** (high-res RGB, thermal, LiDAR, hyperspectral)
- **Ground sensors** (soil moisture, nutrients, microclimate)
- **eDNA sampling** (automated species detection from soil/water)
- **Acoustic monitoring** (bird/insect biodiversity indicators)
- **Camera traps** (wildlife presence and behavior)

All streams feed digital twins that model ecosystem health trajectories.

### 5. Automated MRV (Measurement, Reporting, Verification)
For carbon markets and biodiversity credits:
- **Biomass estimation** from multi-sensor fusion
- **Soil carbon** from spectral analysis + ground truth
- **Biodiversity indices** from eDNA, acoustic, camera data
- **Additionality proof** via counterfactual modeling
- **Permanence risk scoring** with insurance integration
- **Audit-ready reporting** for Verra, Gold Standard, ART

### 6. Indigenous Knowledge Integration
RegenOS is built with indigenous communities, not around them:
- **Traditional ecological knowledge** databases (with permission and benefit-sharing)
- **Community protocols** embedded in restoration plans
- **Revenue sharing** from credits and ecosystem services
- **Local steward networks** for ground implementation
